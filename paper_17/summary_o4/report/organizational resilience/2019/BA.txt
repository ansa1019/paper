Boeing discloses that it maintains an extensive network of technical security controls, policy enforcement mechanisms, monitoring systems and management oversight to address threats to the confidentiality, availability and integrity of its data and systems, and that it also manages information and information technology systems for certain customers who face similar security threats; the company warns that any failure to prevent unauthorized access, release or corruption of its or its customers’ confidential, classified or personally identifiable information could result in significant financial or reputational harm. In addition, Boeing’s fleet support services encompass information services and systems as well as technical data and documents that underpin flight and maintenance training, field service support and engineering services, reflecting its ongoing deployment of IT resources and information management processes to support operational efficiency, user access to accurate and timely data, and the safeguarding of critical technical information.
