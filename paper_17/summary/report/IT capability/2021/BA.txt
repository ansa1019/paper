Boeing maintains an extensive network of technical security controls, policy enforcement mechanisms, monitoring systems and management oversight to address threats to the confidentiality, availability and integrity of its data and systems, and it manages information and information technology systems for certain customers and suppliers; the company warns that business disruptions may include threats to physical security, information technology or cyber-attacks or failures, and that any significant production delays or destruction, manipulation or improper use of Boeing’s or its suppliers’ data, information systems or networks could impact sales, increase expenses and damage the company’s or its products’ reputation; furthermore, Boeing acknowledges that if it cannot prevent unauthorized access, release or corruption of its customers’ and suppliers’ confidential, classified or personally identifiable information, it may suffer reputational harm and financial losses.
