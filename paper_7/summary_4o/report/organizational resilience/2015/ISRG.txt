Open surgery remains the predominant form of surgery, but the large incisions required create trauma to patients, resulting in longer hospitalization and recovery times, which highlights the need for operational flexibility and adaptation in surgical practices. Minimally invasive surgery (MIS) has been widely adopted for certain procedures, demonstrating the ability to absorb strain and improve functioning despite the presence of adversity. The da Vinci surgical systems enable surgeons to extend the benefits of MIS, allowing for a range of motion that mimics human wrist movements while filtering out tremors, thus enhancing surgical precision and operational continuity. Our technology is designed to be easy and safe to use, reflecting a focus on service innovation and resource allocation to meet customer needs effectively.
