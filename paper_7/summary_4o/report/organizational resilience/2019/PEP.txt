To adapt to these challenges, we focus on becoming faster, stronger, and better, which includes winning in the marketplace, transforming our capabilities, and enhancing our sustainability agenda. We continuously monitor the economic, operating, and political environment in markets where we operate to identify actions to mitigate unfavorable impacts on our results. Our integrated risk management framework, overseen by our board of directors, includes identifying, assessing, prioritizing, and addressing top strategic and operational risks. This framework enables us to maintain operational flexibility and resilience in the face of external pressures and disruptions. We leverage technology and scale to enhance our capabilities, which contributes to our organizational strength and competitive advantage. Our risk management strategies involve resource allocation and deployment to ensure business continuity and operational effectiveness. We also emphasize the importance of collaboration networks and inter-organizational collaboration to enhance our adaptability and response to unexpected situations. Our commitment to sustainability and responsible resource management reflects our focus on long-term organizational health and resilience. We actively engage with stakeholders to ensure alignment with customer needs and market demands, which supports our strategic initiatives and future planning. In the context of our operations, we recognize the need for crisis management and preparedness to confront potential hazards and adverse conditions. Our organizational practices are designed to foster a culture of learning and innovation, enabling us to absorb knowledge and adapt to changing market dynamics. We continuously evaluate our performance under pressure and implement strategic responses to maintain operational continuity and effectiveness. Our focus on resource generation and management is critical to navigating complex situations and ensuring the sustainability of our business model. We are committed to leveraging our organizational capabilities and emotional resources to foster a resilient culture that can withstand adversity and capitalize on opportunities for transformation and success.
