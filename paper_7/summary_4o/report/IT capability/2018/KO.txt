The company is also committed to tapping into the passion of its people and building an inclusive culture of curiosity and empowerment. The strategic priorities include digitizing the enterprise, which emphasizes the importance of leveraging technology resources to enhance operational efficiency and market agility. The evolving digital landscape and growth of e-commerce present new challenges that require the company to enhance its IT capabilities to support business needs effectively. The company invests in infrastructure programs that drive volume through increased access to consumers, particularly in emerging markets, and focuses on differentiating its brands in developing markets. The company also emphasizes the importance of responsible water use and sustainability in its operations, recognizing that these factors are critical to its long-term success. The company is committed to innovation and has mobilized its assets in marketing and community outreach to address these challenges.
