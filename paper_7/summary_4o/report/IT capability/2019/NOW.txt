Servicenow's purpose is to make the world of work, work better for people, which involves building applications that help automate existing processes and create efficient, digitized workflows. The company believes that when work flows naturally across disparate departments, systems, and processes, great experiences follow. Servicenow primarily delivers its software via the internet as a service through a simple and easy-to-use interface, allowing for rapid deployment of packaged offerings and enabling customers to build custom applications. The organization continues to invest in the development of its services, infrastructure, and sales and marketing to drive long-term growth. The company provides strategic advisory and consulting services to help customers maximize the value of their Servicenow investment, emphasizing the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities. The firm’s IT capabilities represent the application of physical or intangible IT resources such as technology, knowledge, practices, relationships, management skills, and business process understanding to further organizational goals. The company recognizes the significance of information management capability, which includes providing data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access, tailored to respond to changing business needs and directions. Servicenow's subscription revenues consist of fees that give customers access to the ordered subscription service for both self-hosted offerings and cloud-based subscription offerings, with related support and updates during the subscription term. The company recognizes revenue ratably over the subscription term for cloud-based offerings and a substantial portion of the sales price upon delivery for self-hosted offerings. The increase in subscription revenues is driven by increased purchases by existing customers and an increase in customer count. The organization has seen a significant increase in research and development expenses, primarily due to increased headcount and investments in data center capacity and infrastructure hardware to support its growth. The company expects research and development expenses to continue to increase in absolute dollars as it develops new applications and enhances its core platform. The firm also anticipates that its sales and marketing expenses will increase as it expands its direct sales organization and increases marketing activities. Servicenow's financial performance is influenced by various factors, including foreign currency exchange rates and the timing of contract renewals, which can impact revenues and billings. The company’s operational flexibility and strategic resource allocation are critical for maintaining its competitive advantage in the market. The organization recognizes the importance of leveraging its IT capabilities to support business transformation and improve firm performance, aligning its technology functionalities with business strategy to enhance overall organizational effectiveness.
