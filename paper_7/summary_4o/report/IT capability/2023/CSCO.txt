Cisco designs and sells a broad range of technologies that power the internet, integrating product portfolios across networking, security, collaboration, applications, and the cloud to create highly secure, intelligent platforms for customers' digital businesses. These platforms are designed to help customers manage more users, devices, and things connecting to their networks. In fiscal 2023, Cisco delivered strong results with growth in revenue and profitability, remaining focused on delivering innovation across technologies to assist customers in executing their digital transformations. The company continues to operate in a challenging macroeconomic and highly competitive environment, aggressively investing in priority areas with the objective of driving profitable growth over the long term. The total revenue increased by 11% compared with fiscal 2022, with product revenue increasing by 13% and service revenue by 2%. Total software revenue was $17.0 billion across all product areas and services, an increase of 12%, with subscription revenue increasing by 16%. Cisco's strategy is to securely connect everything, driving a trusted customer experience through innovation, solutions, choice, and people. The company recognizes that as customers add billions of new connections to their enterprises and as more applications move to a multicloud environment, the network becomes even more critical. Cisco's ongoing investment in research and development is aimed at bringing a broad range of products to market in a timely fashion, with R&D expenses increasing due to higher headcount-related expenses and discretionary spending. The company is also focused on optimizing its inventory and supply chain management to ensure competitive lead times while managing the risk of inventory obsolescence due to rapidly changing technology and customer requirements. Cisco's operational flexibility is further enhanced through strategic resource allocation and management capability, which is essential for transforming the firm into a competitive force. The company’s IT capabilities, which encompass the coordination of assets and internal processes, are critical in supporting business needs and enhancing overall firm performance. The emphasis on IT governance and the ability to mobilize and deploy IT-based resources in combination with other capabilities underscores Cisco's commitment to leveraging technology for business innovation and value creation. The firm’s focus on actionable investments and strategic alignment with market dynamics is evident in its approach to managing external relationships and responding to competitive actions. Cisco's commitment to digital transformation and the integration of IT systems is pivotal in navigating the complexities of the current business environment, ensuring that the company remains agile and responsive to customer demands while driving performance improvements across its operations.
