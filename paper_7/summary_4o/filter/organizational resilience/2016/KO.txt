The Coca-Cola Company recognizes that its success depends on the ability to connect with consumers by providing a wide variety of beverage choices to meet their desires, needs, and lifestyle choices. The company is committed to using its formidable assets—brands, financial strength, unrivaled distribution system, global reach, and the talent and strong commitment of its management and associates—to achieve long-term sustainable growth. The company faces numerous challenges and risks, including obesity, water quality and quantity, evolving consumer preferences, increased competition, product safety and quality, and food security, which require effective responses to maintain organizational health and resilience. The company has implemented a formal water risk management program to address water-related risks and has engaged in collaborative efforts with various stakeholders to ensure sustainable water stewardship. The Coca-Cola Company is also focused on enhancing its core capabilities in consumer marketing, commercial leadership, and franchise leadership to adapt to changing market conditions and consumer demands. The company is committed to innovation, which is critical for maintaining competitive advantage and responding effectively to external pressures and disruptive events. The company’s productivity and reinvestment program aims to strengthen its brands and reinvest resources to drive long-term profitable growth, emphasizing operational excellence and resource allocation. The company’s strategic priorities include accelerating growth of a consumer-centric brand portfolio, driving revenue growth, strengthening the Coca-Cola system, digitizing the enterprise, and unlocking the power of its people. The company recognizes the importance of its bottling partners' financial health and success as critical components of its overall performance, and it works collaboratively with them to identify processes that enable quick achievement of scale and efficiencies. In response to challenges, the company has adopted measures to mitigate risks, including the use of hedging activities to manage commodity price risks and fluctuations in foreign currencies. The company’s organizational structure is designed to enhance operational flexibility and adaptability, allowing it to respond effectively to unexpected situations and environmental perturbations. The Coca-Cola Company is also focused on maintaining operational continuity and performance under pressure, ensuring that it can navigate complex situations and market volatility. The company’s commitment to learning and knowledge management supports its ability to absorb strain and preserve functioning despite adversity, reflecting its focus on organizational resilience. The company continuously assesses its operational practices and strategies to ensure alignment with its major objectives and to foster a culture of innovation and adaptability. The Coca-Cola Company’s approach to crisis management and preparedness is integral to its resilience strategies, enabling it to confront challenges and leverage opportunities for growth and sustainability in a turbulent business environment.
