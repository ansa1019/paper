We design, make and sell semiconductors to electronics designers and manufacturers all over the world. Our business model is carefully constructed around attributes that provide a competitive advantage, including the industry's broadest portfolio of differentiated analog and embedded processing semiconductors, which allows us to meet diverse customer needs and generate revenue per system. We invest more than $1 billion each year to develop new products, demonstrating our commitment to innovation and adaptation in response to market demands. Our strong foundation of manufacturing technology and low-cost production enables us to maintain operational flexibility and ensure a consistent supply of products for our customers, which is critical for business continuity. The breadth of our portfolio and market channels provides resilience against dependency on any single product or application, allowing us to absorb strain and preserve functioning despite external pressures. Our global sales force and web presence facilitate unique access to approximately 100,000 customers, enhancing our ability to respond effectively to environmental disruptions. We have a long history of resource allocation towards research and development, which has resulted in increased R&D expenses as we continue to adapt to changing market conditions. Our focus on analog and embedded processing has allowed us to generate strong cash flow from operations, reflecting our organizational health and effectiveness in navigating complex situations. In 2016, cash flows from operations were $4.61 billion, indicating our capability to maintain performance under pressure. The increase in free cash flow to 30.5 percent of revenue underscores our operational resilience and ability to return cash to shareholders, which is a key objective of our strategic initiatives. Our financial condition at the end of 2016, with total cash of $3.49 billion, reflects our preparedness to confront future challenges and sustain operational continuity. We believe we have the necessary financial resources and operating plans to fund our working capital needs and other business requirements for at least the next 12 months, demonstrating our strategic foresight and resilience strategies. The allocation of resources from manufacturing support into R&D activities signifies our commitment to transformation and adaptation in response to evolving customer needs and market dynamics. We continuously assess the impact of external disturbances on our operations and adjust our strategies accordingly, ensuring that we remain agile and capable of thriving in turbulent markets. Our operational management practices emphasize the importance of knowledge management and collaboration networks, which enhance our absorptive capacity and enable us to learn from past experiences. The multifaceted nature of our organizational capabilities allows us to navigate adversity and capitalize on opportunities for growth and success.
