We are a leading provider of transparent and independent ratings, benchmarks, analytics, and data to the capital and commodity markets worldwide. Our operations consist of three reportable segments: ratings, market and commodities intelligence, and S&P Dow Jones indices. We strive for operational excellence, continuous innovation, and a high-performance culture driven by our best-in-class talent. Our strategy includes achieving financial targets and creating shareholder value by focusing on organic revenue growth and continuing to deliver margin expansion with a focus on operating leverage and efficiency opportunities. We aim to deliver greater customer value through deeper client and market insights, innovative solutions, stronger internal teamwork, and reliable, nimble go-to-market processes. Our commitment to a disciplined and practical risk, control, and compliance environment is paramount, as is creating a performance culture to drive innovation, flexibility, and agility to address customer needs. 

The market and commodities intelligence segment's portfolio of capabilities is designed to help the financial community, corporations, and professional service firms track performance, generate better investment returns, identify new trading and investment ideas, perform risk analysis, and develop mitigation strategies. The integration of recent acquisitions has enhanced our analytical capabilities and offerings, allowing us to adapt to changing market conditions and customer needs. Our focus on operational flexibility and strategic adaptation enables us to respond effectively to environmental disruptions and transform our operations to emerge stronger from challenging situations.

We continuously assess our operational resources and capabilities to ensure that we can maintain performance under pressure and adapt to unexpected situations. The implementation of lean management, robotics, automation, and machine learning aims to streamline internal workflows and enhance productivity. Our investments in digital infrastructure capabilities, particularly in cybersecurity, are crucial for maintaining organizational health and resilience. 

We recognize the importance of stakeholder engagement and inter-organizational collaboration in fostering a resilient organizational culture. Our strategic initiatives are designed to enhance planning and software engineering processes, which will speed up the delivery of content and products. We are committed to leadership development programs and skills training to enhance our human capital and ensure that we are prepared for future challenges. 

In 2017, we reported an increase in revenue driven by growth in bank loan ratings and corporate bond ratings, demonstrating our ability to adapt and thrive in a competitive landscape. The successful execution of our strategic priorities reflects our organizational resilience and capacity to confront and benefit from sudden disruptive changes. We believe that our strong financial position, coupled with our proactive measures and strategic foresight, will allow us to navigate future uncertainties and maintain our competitive advantage in the market.
