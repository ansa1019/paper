The following management's discussion and analysis of financial condition and results of operations is intended to help the reader understand the results of operations and financial condition of Microsoft Corporation. Our strategy is to build best-in-class platforms and productivity services for an intelligent cloud and an intelligent edge infused with artificial intelligence. We strive to create local opportunity, growth, and impact in every country around the world, which reflects our commitment to adaptability and resilience in a dynamic and highly competitive industry. Each industry shift is an opportunity to conceive new products, new technologies, or new ideas that can further transform the industry and our business. We must continue to evolve and adapt over an extended time in pace with this changing environment, which underscores the importance of operational flexibility and strategic adaptation. Our success is highly dependent on our ability to attract and retain qualified employees, which is critical for maintaining organizational health and resilience. The investments we are making in infrastructure and devices will continue to increase our operating costs and may decrease our operating margins, highlighting the need for effective resource allocation and risk management strategies. Aggregate demand for our software, services, and devices is correlated to global macroeconomic and geopolitical factors, which remain dynamic and can impact our performance under pressure. Our international operations provide a significant portion of our total revenue and expenses, and many of these are denominated in currencies other than the U.S. dollar, indicating the need for effective management of external pressures and environmental perturbations. We have engaged in strategic initiatives, including acquisitions and restructuring plans, to better focus and align resources, which are essential for enhancing our organizational capabilities and ensuring operational continuity. We are committed to investing in sales, marketing, product support infrastructure, and technology, which are vital for sustaining growth and resilience in turbulent markets. Our ability to foresee and confront challenges, as well as to benefit from sudden disruptive change, is crucial for our survival and success. We continuously evaluate our operational management practices to ensure they align with our major objectives and enhance our organizational effectiveness. The dynamic nature of our industry necessitates a multifaceted approach to resilience, encompassing innovation, collaboration, and strategic foresight. Our financial condition reflects our ongoing commitment to maintaining a robust operational framework that supports our long-term growth and adaptability. We recognize the importance of learning processes and knowledge management in fostering a culture of resilience and agility, enabling us to navigate complex situations and respond effectively to unexpected events. Our focus on strategic resilience and transformation is essential for sustaining our competitive advantage and achieving our organizational goals.
