Mastercard is a technology company in the global payments industry, connecting consumers, financial institutions, merchants, governments, digital partners, businesses, and other organizations worldwide by enabling electronic payments and making those transactions safe, simple, smart, and accessible. Our investments in new networks, such as open banking solutions and digital identity capabilities, support and strengthen our payments and services solutions. Each of our capabilities supports and builds upon each other and are fundamentally interdependent. We employ a multi-layered approach to help protect the global payments ecosystem in which we operate. The increase in net revenue on both an as reported and as adjusted basis was attributable to growth in our payment network and value-added services and solutions. The increase was primarily driven by growth in domestic and cross-border dollar volumes and an increase in the number of switched transactions, reflecting trends of growth in our key drivers. Our value-added services include, among others, cyber and intelligence solutions designed to allow all parties to transact securely, easily, and with confidence, as well as other services that provide proprietary insights, drawing on our principled and responsible use of secure consumer and merchant data. Our investments in technology resources and capabilities are critical for enhancing operational efficiency and supporting business innovation. The core global payments network, combined with our franchise model, sets the standards and ground rules that balance value and risk across all stakeholders and allows for interoperability among them. We recognize that the effective deployment of IT resources is essential for transforming our firm into a competitive force. The growth of our cyber and intelligence solutions is driven by our underlying key drivers and the scaling of our fraud and security solutions. We also focus on the ability to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access, tailoring these in response to changing business needs and directions. Our operational flexibility and strategic resource allocation are vital for responding to market dynamics and consumer demands. The effective management of our technology infrastructure and capabilities is essential for optimizing our business processes and enhancing our organizational effectiveness. The integration of our IT systems and capabilities supports our strategic management practices and enables us to leverage our resources effectively. Our ability to mobilize and deploy IT-based resources in combination with other capabilities is crucial for achieving business value and performance excellence. The transformation of our business processes through technology deployment and innovation partnerships enhances our market responsiveness and competitive positioning. The management of our technology portfolio and the strategic alignment of our IT capabilities with organizational goals are fundamental to our operational success and long-term sustainability.
