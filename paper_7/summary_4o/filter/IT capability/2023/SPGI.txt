We are a provider of credit ratings, benchmarks, analytics, and workflow solutions in the global capital, commodity, and automotive markets. Our operations consist of five businesses: S&P Global Market Intelligence, S&P Global Ratings, S&P Global Commodity Insights, S&P Global Mobility, and S&P Dow Jones Indices. Our strategy focuses on enhancing foundational capabilities, evolving core businesses, and pursuing growth via adjacencies, with an emphasis on delivering an exceptional customer experience. We seek to strengthen data management capabilities for cross-enterprise value creation, ensuring data quality through governance, enhanced architecture, and policy codification. We are adopting efficient modern native cloud technologies and data services, implementing technologies that align with customer needs and unlock new opportunities. Our enterprise-wide AI strategy aims to accelerate innovation in our product offerings and drive productivity with common AI capabilities. We are committed to risk management, compliance, and control across S&P Global, strengthening the security and resiliency of business-critical systems. Our market intelligence segment is a global provider of multi-asset-class data and analytics integrated with purpose-built workflow solutions, designed to help professionals track performance, generate alpha, and manage credit risk. The segment's portfolio includes software and workflow solutions that assist customers in managing and analyzing data, identifying risk, and meeting regulatory requirements. The mobility business serves the full automotive value chain, providing critical information and insights to OEMs, suppliers, and dealerships. We focus on evolving our forecasting business to encompass new technologies and forms of mobility, supporting the industry's transformation to hybrid electric vehicles and digital retail. Our commodity insights segment provides essential price data, analytics, and software services, enabling markets to perform with greater transparency and efficiency. We are subject to increasing regulatory scrutiny, particularly in our commodity insights business, which is governed by benchmark regulations in various jurisdictions. The indices segment maintains a wide variety of valuation and index benchmarks, generating revenue from asset-linked fees and data subscription arrangements. Our technology investments are aimed at developing key product offerings in growth areas such as sustainability and enhancing customer support through integrated capabilities. We recognize the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities to transform the firm into a competitive force. Our IT capabilities encompass the coordination of assets and internal processes, supporting business strategies and work processes. We continue to invest in technology infrastructure and capabilities to improve operational efficiency and enhance our organizational effectiveness. The evolving legal and regulatory environment presents both challenges and opportunities for our business, necessitating ongoing adaptation and investment in compliance and technology management.
