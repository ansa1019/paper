We are a leading provider of transparent and independent ratings, benchmarks, analytics, and data to the capital and commodity markets worldwide. Our operations consist of four reportable segments: S&P Global Ratings, S&P Global Market Intelligence, S&P Global Platts, and S&P Dow Jones Indices. In 2018, we announced the launch of "Powering the Markets of the Future" to provide a framework for our forward-looking business strategy, which seeks to deliver an exceptional, differentiated customer experience by enhancing our foundational capabilities, evolving and growing our core businesses, and pursuing growth via adjacencies. Our strategy includes funding organic opportunities with continued productivity gains and pursuing a disciplined acquisition, investment, and partnership strategy to support our strategic initiatives. We aim to modernize and enhance the delivery of our products across multiple channels and create an inclusive performance-driven culture that drives employee engagement and aligns with our purpose of accelerating progress in the world. In 2019, we recorded a pre-tax gain related to the sale of Rigdata and SPIAS, which reflects our ongoing efforts to optimize our portfolio and enhance our operational efficiency. Our market intelligence segment includes capabilities designed to help investment professionals track performance, generate alpha, identify investment ideas, and assess credit risk. We focus on leveraging new technology and data capabilities to transform our value chain and enhance our customer experience. Our technology costs have increased, reflecting our commitment to modernizing our workplace and improving end-user productivity. We also prioritize reducing our cybersecurity risk while augmenting process maturity. The acquisition of Kensho Technologies has further strengthened our analytics and data visualization capabilities, enabling us to better serve our customers and enhance our competitive advantage. Our commitment to technology integration and operational flexibility is evident in our investments in technology infrastructure and data management solutions, which are critical to meeting evolving business needs and enhancing our market responsiveness. We continue to focus on innovation partnerships and strategic management practices to drive performance improvement and actionable options in our business processes. Our ability to mobilize and deploy IT-based resources in combination with other capabilities has become essential in transforming our firm into a competitive force. We are committed to maintaining a strong risk, internal control, and compliance environment, which is vital for our organizational effectiveness and agility in a rapidly changing business landscape. Our focus on strategic resource allocation and capability enhancement is designed to create value and improve our overall performance outcomes. We recognize the importance of information management capability, ensuring that we provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, and security. Our operational decisions are guided by a strategic alignment with our organizational goals, enabling us to respond effectively to market dynamics and external influences.
