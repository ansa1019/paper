Our MD&A discloses that we are principally engaged in advanced technology systems, products and services “in defense, space, intelligence, homeland security and information technology, including cybersecurity,” and that we “provide a broad range of … system integration and cybersecurity services.”  We emphasize that “key to enabling success of our strategy is … executing on our multi-year business transformation initiative to enhance our digital infrastructure and increase efficiencies and collaboration throughout our business,” and we “invest substantially in our people to ensure that our workforce has the technical skills necessary to succeed, and we expect to continue to invest internally in innovative technologies that address rapidly evolving mission requirements for our customers.”  We also disclose that our capital expenditures “are comprised of equipment and facilities infrastructure and information technology (inclusive of costs for the development or purchase of internal-use software that are capitalized),” and that we incur IT-related capital expenditures “to support programs and general enterprise information technology infrastructure, inclusive of costs for the development or purchase of internal-use software.”
