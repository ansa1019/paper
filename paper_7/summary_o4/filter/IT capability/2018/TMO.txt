The company’s provisions for income taxes, including adjustments under the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, establishment of valuation allowances against net operating losses and foreign tax credit planning, reflect systematic risk assessment and regulatory adaptability. Moreover, accruals for contingencies, environmental liabilities, indemnifications and guarantees, together with rigorous critical accounting policies and estimates for goodwill, intangible assets and pension obligations, illustrate the firm’s ability to absorb strain, maintain system integrity and preserve or improve functioning under nominal, extreme or exceptional conditions.
