The disclosure outlines the allocation of net income between controlling and noncontrolling interests in Accenture’s subsidiary entities, specifying that net income attributable to noncontrolling interests reflects income earned or expenses incurred by equity interests held by certain current and former members of Accenture leadership and permitted transferees in Accenture Canada Holdings Inc., as well as by noncontrolling shareholders in Avanade Inc., with net income attributable to Accenture plc representing the amount due to its shareholders, all in accordance with the company’s business-organizational structure.
