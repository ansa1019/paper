Net income attributable to noncontrolling interests reflects the income earned or expense incurred by the equity interests held by certain current and former members of Accenture leadership and their permitted transferees in our Accenture Holdings plc and Accenture Canada Holdings Inc. subsidiaries, and since January 2002 has also included immaterial amounts primarily attributable to noncontrolling shareholders in our Avanade Inc. subsidiary, while net income attributable to Accenture plc represents the income attributable to the shareholders of Accenture plc; this disclosure directs readers to the business-organizational structure section, underscoring the manner in which equity interests and organizational design are structured across consolidated entities.
