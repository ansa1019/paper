Net income attributable to noncontrolling interests reflects the income earned or expense incurred attributable to the equity interest that certain current and former members of Accenture leadership and their permitted transferees hold in our Accenture Canada Holdings Inc. subsidiary, as further described under “business-organizational structure,” and also includes amounts primarily attributable to noncontrolling shareholders in our Avanade Inc. subsidiary; by delineating the allocation of financial resources and obligations across distinct legal entities, this disclosure underscores the company’s approach to distributing economic returns and sustaining operational continuity across its organizational structure, while net income attributable to Accenture plc represents the income attributable to the shareholders of Accenture plc.
