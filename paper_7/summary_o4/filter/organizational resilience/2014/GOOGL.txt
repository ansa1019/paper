The Company continuously adapts to a dynamic offline-to-online and multi-device environment by expanding its products and services to maintain and grow the business in response to evolving user behaviors and advertising trends, invests heavily in research and development and capital expenditures to strengthen its systems, data centers and IT infrastructure, and pursues strategic acquisitions that enhance the breadth and depth of its offerings and engineering expertise. The Company regularly assesses and adjusts reserves for uncertain tax positions and legal contingencies, performs impairment reviews of goodwill, long-lived assets and marketable securities when events or changes in circumstances indicate potential loss, and allocates resources globally to preserve performance under external pressures such as evolving user preferences, competitive dynamics and economic conditions. Ongoing investments in workforce expansion, competitive compensation programs and professional services, together with rigorous monitoring of contractual obligations and capital lease commitments, support its capacity to absorb strain and preserve or improve functioning despite adversity, consistent with the maintenance of positive adjustment under challenging conditions.
