The corporation’s 2017 MD&A describes substantial investments in its digital infrastructure and self-service channels, noting industry-leading digital banking platforms (www.bankofamerica.com) with approximately 35 million active users, including roughly 24 million mobile active users, to deliver secure connectivity and tailored online services across its consumer and small-business networks. It highlights continued expansion of digital capabilities—such as enhancements to online and mobile banking, growth in self-service options and the development of a self-directed online investing platform through Merrill Edge—designed to improve operational flexibility, customer engagement and responsiveness to evolving consumer preferences. The report further notes that digital banking active users increased by millions in 2017, reflecting shifts in customer behavior toward online channels, and emphasizes that these efforts have been complemented by an optimized physical network to leverage IT-driven efficiency and support the firm’s strategic growth objectives.
