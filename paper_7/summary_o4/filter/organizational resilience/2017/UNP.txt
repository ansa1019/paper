Union Pacific continues to mobilize technology‐based resources to support and enhance its operations, expressly “utilizing technology, risk assessment, training and employee engagement, quality control, and targeted capital investments” to drive its total safety culture, deploying and testing positive train control across a growing number of routes in the network, and planning “significant investments in technology improvements … including PTC” within its 2018 capital plan; the company aligns resources with customer demand to maintain network fluidity and surge capability, leverages data-driven fuel surcharge programs that trail market price changes by approximately two months, reports weekly key performance measures to the Association of American Railroads and publishes this information on its website, and maintains digital infrastructure and information management processes to support capital planning, performance measurement, operational flexibility, and transparent stakeholder reporting.
