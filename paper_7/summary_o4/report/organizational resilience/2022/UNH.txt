UnitedHealth Group describes its ongoing deployment of data-driven capabilities and information systems to support and enhance its care delivery and management strategies, noting that it uses data and analytics to furnish clinicians with the necessary information to deliver cost-efficient, high-quality care and has directed investments toward new clinical and administrative information and management systems to enable value-based care models and integration of its care delivery platforms. The company further highlights growth in its Optum Insight segment “in technology and managed services,” reflecting expansion of its technology-enabled offerings and partnerships with health systems. Additionally, UnitedHealth Group reports reinvestment of operating cash flows through capital expenditures in property, equipment and capitalized software, underscoring continued augmentation of IT resources and infrastructure to support its business strategies, operational flexibility and future growth.
