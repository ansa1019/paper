Danaher’s MD&A highlights rapid technological development—particularly in computing, automation, artificial intelligence, mobile connectivity, communications and digitization—as both an opportunity and challenge in its diverse global markets, and notes that the company is making significant investments, organically and through acquisitions, to address this technological change and to globalize its manufacturing, research and development and customer-facing resources in order to improve responsiveness to customer needs and operational efficiency worldwide. Individual businesses monitor key competitors and customer sales data to gauge relative performance and future outlook, reflecting robust information management and analytical capabilities. Throughout the COVID-19 pandemic, Danaher continues to actively monitor variant spread and governmental responses, has implemented and is regularly updating business continuity plans to mitigate risks to employees, customers, partners, manufacturing capacity and supply chains, and is leveraging its capabilities and scale to develop and deploy rapid diagnostic tests. The company’s capital expenditures, which increased in 2021, include improvements to information technology systems alongside investments in manufacturing capacity and new product development, underscoring the importance of strengthening IT infrastructure to support strategic objectives and enhance operational agility.
