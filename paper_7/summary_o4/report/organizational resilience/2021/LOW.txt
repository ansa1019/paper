In fiscal 2021, we enhanced the customer online shopping experience with improved search and navigation functionality and expanded on-trend inventory assortments, expanded our omnichannel fulfillment capabilities by converting three geographic areas to a market-based delivery model for big and bulky products that flow directly from our distribution network to customer homes, enabled real-time tracking of appliance deliveries and broadened same-day and next-day fulfillment options, launched a new store inventory management system that provides associates with real-time visibility into store inventory and reduces non-productive hours, and rolled out a simplified user interface across the sales floor to accelerate associate training, enable greater focus on customer service and reduce customer wait times; looking forward to 2022, we intend to further enhance our omnichannel capabilities by expanding the Lowes.com assortment, upgrading online visualization and configuration tools and improving fulfillment.
