Danaher’s MD&A highlights rapid technological development—particularly in computing, automation, artificial intelligence, mobile connectivity, communications and digitization—in most of its served markets and describes significant investments, both organic and acquisition-based, to address this pace of change by globalizing its manufacturing, research and development and customer-facing resources to enhance responsiveness and improve operational efficiency. The company reports that capital expenditures are typically made for increasing manufacturing capacity, supporting new product development and improving information technology systems, and notes that professional fees associated with regulatory filings and separation activities included work within information technology functions. Danaher further emphasizes ongoing productivity improvement initiatives and process enhancements, including digital and automation deployments, as key drivers of cost savings, operational leverage and the effective deployment of technology-based resources in support of its strategic business objectives.
