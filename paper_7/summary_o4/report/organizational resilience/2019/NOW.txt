ServiceNow’s management discussion and analysis describes a strategy of continuously developing, deploying and reconfiguring its technology resources to enable business processes, inter-departmental workflows and interfirm relationships, underscoring its evolving IT capability. The company “builds applications that help automate existing processes and create efficient, digitized workflows” and “primarily deliver[s] our software via the internet as a service through a simple and easy-to-use interface so that we can rapidly deploy our packaged offerings, and customers can easily build their custom applications,” reflecting investments in digital technologies, connectivity and operational flexibility. Subscription revenues from cloud-based offerings are recognized ratably over the subscription term, while self-hosted offerings treat software and related support as distinct performance obligations, demonstrating IT architectural competence. The firm “continues to invest in the development of our services, infrastructure and sales and marketing to drive long-term growth,” with cost of subscription revenues principally comprising “data center capacity costs,” “depreciation related to our infrastructure hardware equipment dedicated for customer use,” “software, IT services and support dedicated for customer use,” and “personnel-related costs directly associated with data center operations and customer support,” evidencing resource allocation to ensure reliability, security and performance. Research and development expenses likewise include “data center capacity and depreciation costs” and “costs associated with outside services contracted for research and development purposes,” demonstrating sustained investment in platform innovation and technology functionalities. Deferred commissions and referral fees are capitalized and amortized over customer contracts, illustrating strategic resource allocation to support customer acquisition and retention capabilities. Capital expenditures on infrastructure hardware, leasehold improvements and intangible assets further underscore ServiceNow’s commitment to expanding its IT infrastructure and organizational capability to streamline processes, enhance information management capability and support ongoing business transformation.
