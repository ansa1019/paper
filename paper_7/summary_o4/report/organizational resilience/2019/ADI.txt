The discussion of revenue trends by end market notes that product categorization “is determined using a variety of data points including the technical characteristics of the product, the ‘sold to’ customer information, the ‘ship to’ customer information and the end customer product or application into which our product will be incorporated,” and that “as data systems for capturing and tracking this data and our methodology evolves and improves, the categorization of products by end market can vary over time,” leading to reclassifications of prior periods. In describing sales channels, the Company states that it sells products globally through a direct sales force, third-party distributors, independent sales representatives and “via our website,” indicating reliance on digital infrastructure to provide connectivity and access. In its liquidity and capital resources section, the Company explains that it “continually monitor[s] the amount of credit exposure to any one issuer and diversify[s] our investments in order to minimize our credit risk,” suggesting the deployment of monitoring systems and information management capabilities. Finally, in the research and development discussion, the Company discloses that it has “hundreds of R&D projects underway,” expects to “continue the development of innovative technologies and processes for new products,” and believes “a continued commitment to R&D is essential to maintain product leadership with our existing products as well as to provide innovative new product offerings,” reflecting ongoing investments in technology resources, capability enhancement and process innovation to support its strategic objectives.
