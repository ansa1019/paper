Fiserv provides account processing and digital banking solutions; card issuer processing and network services; payments; e-commerce; merchant acquiring and processing; and the Clover® cloud-based point-of-sale solution, and its fintech segment provides technology solutions that enable deposit and loan account processing, central information file management, digital banking, financial and risk management, cash management, professional services and consulting, and item processing and source capture services; examples of these solutions include electronic payments and delivery methods such as internet, mobile and tablet banking as financial institutions modernize their technology, expand the use of data and migrate from in-house technology to outsourced solutions to enhance customer experience, regulatory compliance and operational efficiency; following the acquisition of First Data, Fiserv continues to implement post-merger integration plans to achieve enhanced revenue growth from expanded capabilities, streamlined operations and operational efficiency synergies through alignment of IT resources and applications; acquisitions of MerchantPro Express LLC, Bypass Mobile LLC, Inlet LLC and Ondot Systems, Inc. further expand the company’s portfolio of enterprise POS, omni-commerce, secure digital delivery and digital experience platforms to enable seamless omnichannel commerce and frictionless, digital-first personalized experiences; Fiserv’s revenue recognition policies address processing and services revenue generated from data and transaction processing, electronic billing and payment services, consulting and software maintenance, and product revenue from card and print production, software license sales, hardware (POS devices) sales and significant customization or hosted software arrangements, each accounted for as distinct performance obligations with allocation based on relative standalone selling prices, estimates of variable consideration and evaluation of contract modifications; capital expenditures, including capitalized software development costs, represented approximately 6% and 7% of total revenue in 2020 and 2019, respectively; and to maintain business continuity during the COVID-19 pandemic Fiserv has required a majority of employees to work remotely, enhanced facility security and hygiene protocols, and deployed personal protective equipment and connectivity measures.
