Management’s Discussion and Analysis underscores the Company’s strategic response to a rapidly evolving digital landscape, noting that the growth of e-commerce and mobile device applications has led to dramatic shifts in consumer shopping habits and created new challenges to maintain brand competitiveness and relevance. The Company reports that it is continuously strengthening its capabilities in marketing and innovation to compete in a digital environment and maintain brand loyalty and market share, including increasing investments in e-commerce to support retail and meal-delivery services, offering package sizes fit-for-purpose for online sales and shifting more consumer and trade promotions to digital. As one of its strategic priorities, the Company cites “winning in the marketplace with aligned data-driven revenue growth management and execution capabilities,” reflecting an emphasis on information management, analytics and connectivity to tailor offerings and adjust to changing business needs. It further notes that its success depends on its ability to connect with consumers by providing a wide variety of beverage options and on the ability of its people to execute effectively every day, underpinned by ongoing investments in its portfolio of brands, strategic capabilities and people, thereby implying a coordinated deployment of technology resources and processes to enhance operational flexibility, market responsiveness and competitive advantage.
