Danaher’s management highlights that the company operates in a highly competitive environment characterized by rapid technological development—particularly in computing, automation, artificial intelligence, mobile connectivity, communications and digitization—which drives its continued investments in products, software and services to support research, discovery, development and manufacturing workflows. To address this pace of technological change, Danaher is making significant organic and acquisitive investments to globalize its manufacturing, research and development and customer-facing resources, thereby enhancing responsiveness to worldwide customers and improving the efficiency of its operations. The company’s focus on innovation and differentiated new product development with higher margins is underpinned by continued productivity improvement initiatives, deployment of advanced instrumentation and software, and strategic R&D spending—consisting principally of internal and contract engineering personnel costs—designed to optimize processes, leverage technology-based resources and support business strategies across its global value chain.
