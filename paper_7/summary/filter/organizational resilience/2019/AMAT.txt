Applied provides manufacturing equipment, services and software to the semiconductor, display and related industries, working closely with its global customers to design systems and processes that meet their planned technical and production requirements. Its Applied Global Services segment offers integrated solutions to optimize equipment and fab performance and productivity—including spares, upgrades, services, remanufactured earlier-generation equipment and factory automation software—leveraging the installed base of systems and comprehensive service agreements to deliver performance gains, operational flexibility and improved yields. At the corporate level, operating expenses include unabsorbed information technology and occupancy, and management underscores the critical need for continued, substantial investments in research, development and engineering to deliver innovative technology ahead of customer demand and sustain a competitive advantage. Applied’s financial and liquidity management—including its accounts receivable securitization programs, discounting of letters of credit, credit facilities, revolving credit agreement, term loan credit agreement for acquisition financing and senior unsecured notes—relies on rigorous information management and monitoring of days sales outstanding, cash flows, portfolio credit risk and working capital to support strategic resource allocation and business transformation.
