The annual report highlights the importance of our technology resources and information systems capability to support and secure our operations, noting in particular “the ability of our technology resources, including information systems and security, to perform as anticipated such as with respect to preventing material information technology failures or third-party infiltrations or hacking resulting in consequences adverse to ACE or its customers or partners.” It further describes ongoing reviews and validations of pricing‐service methodologies and fair-value inputs—controls designed to ensure information accuracy and reliability—and references an internal compliance system that monitors portfolio diversification limits and adherence to contractual investment guidelines, reflecting our capability to deploy and leverage IT-enabled processes in support of decision-making, risk management, and regulatory compliance.
