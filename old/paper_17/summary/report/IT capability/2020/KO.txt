The company relies on networks and information systems to support a variety of business processes and activities, including procurement and supply chain, manufacturing, distribution, invoicing and collection of payments, employee processes, consumer marketing, mergers and acquisitions, and research and development. Information systems are critical to many of the company's operating activities, and the business may be impacted by system shutdowns, service disruptions, or security breaches. The company has made investments in personnel, technologies, cyber insurance, and training of personnel to address risks to information systems. The digital evolution is affecting how the company interacts with consumers, customers, suppliers, bottlers, and other business partners. The company believes that its future success will depend in part on its ability to adapt to and thrive in the digital environment, which includes creating more relevant and personalized experiences, developing powerful digital tools for retail customers, and digitizing operations through data, artificial intelligence, automation, and robotics. The company has established a program to manage information risk, which is supervised by information technology management and reviewed by a cross-functional committee. The company is subject to various laws and regulations relating to personal data, which impose operational requirements and may result in significant penalties for noncompliance. The company is committed to ensuring compliance with personal data protection and privacy laws to avoid reputational damage and regulatory enforcement actions. The company's ability to successfully manage third-party relationships is critical, as it relies on third-party service providers for key portions of its operations. The company has implemented procedures for selecting and managing these relationships, but it does not have control over their business operations, which increases financial, legal, reputational, and operational risk. The company recognizes the importance of enhancing its core capabilities in consumer marketing, commercial leadership, and franchise leadership to support growth. The company focuses on understanding customer needs and ensuring that customers have the right product and package offerings. The financial health and success of bottling partners are critical components of the company's success, and the company works with them to identify processes that enable quick achievement of scale and efficiencies. The company acknowledges the challenges posed by the rapidly evolving digital landscape and the need to continuously strengthen capabilities in marketing and innovation to maintain brand loyalty and market share. The company has identified key objectives to navigate the pandemic and position itself to emerge stronger, including optimizing its portfolio of strong brands, establishing a disciplined innovation framework, and enhancing data-driven revenue growth management capabilities. The company is committed to investing in new capabilities to support its accelerated strategy and to adapt to the changing business environment.
