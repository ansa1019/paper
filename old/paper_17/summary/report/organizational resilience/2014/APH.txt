The company focuses its research and development efforts through close collaboration with its OEM customers to develop highly-engineered products that meet customer needs. The company has a strategy to provide comprehensive design capabilities, a broad selection of products, and a high level of service while maintaining continuing programs of productivity improvement and cost control. The company may experience difficulties and unanticipated expenses in assimilating newly acquired businesses, which could affect its operational continuity. The company is subject to various risks, including political, economic, and military instability in countries outside the United States, which could negatively affect sales, profitability, or liquidity. Changes in defense expenditures may also reduce the company's sales, as a significant portion of its revenue is derived from military markets. The company believes that its ability to generate cash from operations is a fundamental financial strength that provides flexibility to meet financial commitments. The company is exposed to risks that could negatively affect its business, including unforeseen interruptions resulting from strikes, financial instabilities, or natural disasters. The company has a history of borrowing funds domestically and continues to have the ability to borrow at reasonable interest rates. The company’s strategic objective is to enhance its position in its served markets by pursuing broad diversification, developing performance-enhancing interconnect solutions, expanding its global presence, controlling costs, and fostering collaborative management. The company’s results may be negatively affected by changing interest rates, foreign currency exchange rates, and general economic conditions. The company’s ability to adapt to changing market conditions and customer needs is vital for its continued success and resilience in the face of external pressures and disruptions.
