Occidental's strategy focuses on delivering a unique shareholder value proposition through continual enhancements to its asset quality, organizational capability, and innovative technical applications that provide competitive advantages. The company aims to maximize shareholder returns through a combination of consistent dividend growth, allocating capital to high-return opportunities across the integrated business, and maintaining a strong balance sheet to secure business and enhance shareholder value. Occidental conducts its operations with a focus on sustainability, health, safety, and environmental and social responsibility, accepting commodity, engineering, and limited exploration risks while seeking to limit its financial and political risks. The company positions itself to thrive in both up- and down-cycle commodity price environments, emphasizing operational flexibility and the ability to adapt to market volatility. Occidental's oil and gas segment implements its strategy by operating and developing areas where reserves are known to exist, maintaining a disciplined approach to capital expenditures, and focusing on cost-reduction efficiencies and improvement in new well productivity. The company utilizes enhanced oil recovery techniques in mature fields and emphasizes the importance of collaboration with commercial and governmental entities to bring additional production quickly to market. Occidental's midstream and marketing segment strives to maximize realized value by optimizing the use of its pipeline and export capacities and providing access to domestic and international markets. The segment evaluates opportunities across the value chain and uses its assets to provide services to Occidental subsidiaries and third parties, thereby enhancing operational continuity and resilience. Occidental's chemical segment aims to generate cash flow in excess of its normal capital expenditure requirements, achieving above-cost-of-capital returns by being a low-cost producer.
