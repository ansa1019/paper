Occidental's strategy focuses on delivering a unique shareholder value proposition through continual enhancements to its asset quality, organizational capability, and innovative technical applications that provide competitive advantages. The company aims to maximize shareholder returns by maintaining a sustainable and sector-leading dividend, allocating capital to high-return, short-cycle and long-cycle, cash-flow generating opportunities across its integrated business, and generating free cash flow growth to reduce debt and return cash to shareholders. Occidental conducts its operations with a focus on sustainability, health, safety, and environmental and social responsibility, positioning the business to thrive in both up- and down-cycle commodity price environments. The integration of newly acquired assets is expected to deliver lower breakeven costs and generate excess free cash flow, enhancing operational flexibility and resilience. Occidental's diversified portfolio provides numerous competitive advantages, allowing the company to bring additional production quickly to market and extend the life of older fields at lower costs. The company emphasizes the importance of maintaining operational continuity and adapting to external pressures, including environmental perturbations and market volatility. Occidental's oil and gas segment implements strategies to achieve low development and operating costs, focusing on cost-reduction efficiencies and innovative technologies to reduce carbon emissions. The company also seeks to capitalize on its leadership in enhanced oil recovery by developing carbon capture, utilization, and storage projects, which are integral to its long-term strategy for sustainability and resilience. Occidental's management practices include a disciplined approach to capital expenditures, focusing on high-return opportunities while maintaining a strong balance sheet to secure business and enhance shareholder value. The company recognizes the need for adaptability in response to changing market conditions and emphasizes the importance of strategic initiatives and inter-organizational collaboration to navigate complex situations. Occidental's commitment to operational excellence is reflected in its focus on continuous improvement and learning processes, ensuring that the organization can effectively confront challenges and leverage opportunities for growth. The company is also aware of the risks associated with its offshore operations, including environmental hazards and the impacts of climate change, and has established mechanisms for risk assessment and management to mitigate these challenges. By fostering a culture of resilience and agility, Occidental aims to maintain its organizational health and effectiveness in the face of adversity, ensuring its ability to thrive in a turbulent business environment.
