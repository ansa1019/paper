The Allstate Corporation board of directors has overall responsibility for oversight of management's design and implementation of the enterprise risk and return management framework that manages the business on an integrated basis following risk and return principles. The risk and return committee oversees the effectiveness of the program, governance structure, and risk-related decision-making while focusing on the company's overall risk profile. The company operates in highly competitive markets and must continually allocate resources to refine and improve products and services to maintain its reputation, enhance brand perception, and remain competitive. The ability to attract, develop, and retain talent to maintain appropriate staffing levels and establish a successful work culture is critical to success. Competition for qualified employees with highly specialized knowledge in areas such as underwriting, data and analytics, technology, and e-commerce is intense. The unexpected loss of key personnel could have a material adverse impact on the business due to the loss of their skills and industry experience. The company is subject to significant risks, including strategic, insurance, investment, financial, operational, and culture risks, which are managed through an integrated framework that includes governance, processes, culture, and activities performed on an enterprise-wide basis. The company regularly identifies, measures, manages, monitors, and reports all significant risks. The enterprise risk appetite is cascaded into individual risk limits, which set boundaries on the amount of risk the company is willing to accept from specific risk categories. The company employs various strategies to manage risks, including scenario analysis and sensitivity testing to gauge the robustness of its risk, capital, and liquidity positions. The company’s approach to managing operational risk includes ensuring the effectiveness of internal controls over financial reporting and disclosure controls. The company has established a comprehensive risk management culture that emphasizes the identification, assessment, management, monitoring, and reporting of risks. Changes in market conditions, including economic and capital market conditions, could adversely affect the company’s business and results of operations. The company’s ability to respond effectively to environmental disruptions and transform itself to emerge stronger from challenging situations is integral to its operational flexibility and resilience. The company’s strategic initiatives focus on enhancing customer value, improving operational efficiency, and adapting to changing consumer preferences and technological advancements. The company’s risk management strategies adapt to changes in business and market environments, ensuring a proactive approach to risk mitigation and recovery from adverse events. The company’s governance structure supports timely discussion, escalation, and prioritization of issues, ensuring that resilience strategies are effectively implemented across all levels of the organization. The company’s commitment to maintaining operational continuity and positive adjustment under challenging conditions is reflected in its comprehensive risk management practices and strategic foresight.
