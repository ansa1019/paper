Our business is complex, and we depend heavily on suppliers and other third parties for efficient operation. Disruptions with respect to such suppliers, or their failure to perform adequately, could have a material adverse effect on our financial performance. We must provide our customers with reliable, trustworthy service and protect the communications, location, and personal information shared or generated by our customers. Our ability to generate cash in the future, which is subject to these operational risks, will determine our capacity to make payments on our debt and fund significant planned capital expenditures. Our financial performance is sensitive to changes in general economic conditions, including interest rates and consumer credit conditions, which may adversely affect our business and financial performance. We must maintain a strong focus on retaining customers through our initiatives and ongoing network improvements, as our ability to attract and retain branded customers is crucial for revenue generation.
