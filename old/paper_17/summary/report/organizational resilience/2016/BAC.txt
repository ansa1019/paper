In the course of conducting our business operations, we are exposed to a variety of risks that could affect our businesses, results of operations, and financial condition. We manage risks through a comprehensive approach that includes the identification, measurement, monitoring, and control of key risks. Our risk management framework is designed to minimize risk and loss, and we seek to maintain a strong and flexible financial position to withstand challenging economic conditions. Our operational risk management program addresses the overarching processes for identifying, measuring, monitoring, and controlling operational risk, which includes legal risk. We continuously update our systems to support operations and growth, and we have implemented backup systems and safeguards to mitigate the impact of significant disruptions. In times of market stress, we may experience correlations among previously uncorrelated indicators, which can limit the effectiveness of our hedging strategies and lead to significant losses. Our liquidity risk management aims to meet expected and unexpected cash flow needs, and we maintain a diversified funding strategy to enhance resilience against market volatility. We utilize various models and strategies to assess and control our market risk exposures, which are subject to inherent limitations. Our risk management processes include stress testing to evaluate potential impacts on our capital and liquidity under adverse scenarios. The ability to adapt to changing market conditions is critical, and we continuously monitor our risk profile to ensure alignment with our strategic objectives. Our organizational practices emphasize the importance of a sound risk culture, which is fundamental to our values and operating principles. We have developed contingency plans to prepare for adverse economic or market stress, ensuring that we can respond effectively to unexpected situations. The board and its committees provide oversight of our risk management activities, ensuring that we maintain an acceptable risk profile. Our strategic initiatives are aligned with our risk appetite, and we regularly review capital allocations to support business continuity. The corporation's ability to absorb strain and preserve functioning despite adversity is reflected in our proactive measures to manage risks associated with credit, market, liquidity, compliance, operational, and reputational factors. We are committed to maintaining operational flexibility and adaptability in response to environmental perturbations, ensuring that we can navigate complex situations and sustain organizational health. Our approach to risk management is multifaceted, incorporating insights from various hierarchical levels within the organization, and we emphasize the importance of inter-organizational collaboration to enhance resilience. The corporation's strategic resilience is supported by a focus on resource allocation, knowledge management, and the development of adaptive strategies to confront challenges. We recognize the significance of emotional resources and social capital in fostering a culture of resilience, which is essential for organizational survival and success in turbulent markets.
