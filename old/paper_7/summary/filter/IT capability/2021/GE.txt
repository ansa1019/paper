The COVID-19 pandemic has impacted global economies, resulting in workforce and travel restrictions, supply chain and production disruptions, and reduced demand and spending across many sectors. The pandemic has had a material adverse impact on operations and financial performance, particularly in the aviation segment. The company has announced plans to form three public companies focused on aviation, healthcare, and energy, which will allow for greater focus, tailored capital allocation, and strategic flexibility to deliver long-term growth and create value for customers, investors, and employees. The aviation business is committed to investment in developing and maturing technologies that enable a more sustainable future of flight, including a technology development program targeting lower fuel consumption and CO2 emissions. The healthcare segment is focused on creating new products and digital solutions tailored to the different needs of global customers, with an emphasis on improving efficiency in healthcare delivery and expanding access to advanced healthcare. The renewable energy segment benefits from a broad portfolio that positions it to lead the energy transition, with a focus on products, services, and integrated solutions to grow renewable energy generation and modernize the grid. The company continues to invest in generating product improvements and digital strategies to add value for customers looking for clean, renewable energy. The power segment is focused on streamlining its business to align with market demand and drive operational rigor, with an emphasis on risk management and commercial selectivity to secure deals that meet financial hurdles. The company’s digital strategy and investments in technical innovation are aimed at enhancing operational efficiency and supporting business transformation. The organization is also committed to research and development activities to enhance existing products and develop new offerings to meet changing customer needs and market opportunities. The management of information technology resources is critical for supporting business needs and enhancing operational capabilities, with an emphasis on mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging other strategic resources. The company’s approach to information management capability focuses on providing data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access, while tailoring these capabilities in response to changing business needs and directions. The organization recognizes the importance of effective IT governance and resource allocation in achieving competitive advantage and operational flexibility. The company continues to monitor the impacts of external influences, including regulatory dynamics and market restructuring, on its strategic resource allocation and investment decisions, ensuring alignment with organizational goals and market-based performance.
