KDP has invested significantly in state-of-the-art manufacturing and warehousing facilities to optimize its supply chain network through integration and productivity projects. The company continues to manage all aspects of its business, including monitoring the financial health of customers, suppliers, and other third-party relationships, implementing gross margin enhancement strategies through integration and productivity initiatives, and developing new opportunities for growth such as innovation and agreements with partners to distribute brands that are accretive to its portfolio. KDP's ability to navigate these challenges is supported by its broad portfolio and unmatched distribution network, which positions it to manage its business effectively.
