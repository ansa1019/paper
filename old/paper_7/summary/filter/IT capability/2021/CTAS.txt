Cintas helps more than one million businesses of all types and sizes get ready to open their doors with confidence every day by providing a wide range of products and services that enhance customers' image and help keep their facilities and employees clean, safe, and looking their best. Cintas' principal objective is to exceed customers' expectations in order to maximize long-term value for shareholders and working partners, providing a framework for its business strategy. This strategy aims to achieve revenue growth by increasing penetration at existing customers and broadening the customer base to include market segments not historically served. The company has a highly talented and diverse team of service professionals visiting customers regularly, enabling the development of close personal relationships. The combination of the distribution system and strong customer relationships provides a platform for launching additional products and services. Cintas evaluates operating segment performance based on revenue and income before income taxes, regularly reviewing its operating segments for reporting purposes based on information its chief operating decision maker regularly reviews for resource allocation and performance assessment. The COVID-19 pandemic impacted the ability to conduct normal business operations, affecting customer relationships and operational efficiency. The company increased inventory in response to customer needs and demand associated with safety and cleanliness requirements during the pandemic. Cintas' revenue growth was positively impacted by new business and sales productivity increases, as well as the penetration of additional products and services into existing customers. The company believes its cash flows from operating activities will remain sufficient to provide adequate levels of short-term liquidity, with access to a revolving credit facility. Cintas' investments in technology resources and capabilities are essential for supporting business strategies and enhancing operational flexibility. The firm’s IT capabilities, which encompass the effective mobilization and deployment of IT-based resources, are critical in transforming Cintas into a competitive force. The company’s strategic management practices involve evaluating investment opportunities and resource allocation to enhance operational efficiency and market responsiveness. Cintas' financial activities, including capital expenditures and acquisitions, are aligned with its business strategy to support growth and expansion opportunities. The firm’s ability to leverage technology functionalities and integrate IT systems is vital for maintaining competitive advantage and improving performance metrics. The ongoing evaluation of strategic decisions related to technology deployment and organizational capabilities is essential for achieving business transformation and optimizing internal processes. Cintas' commitment to innovation partnerships and strategic resource allocation further supports its objectives in a dynamic business environment.
