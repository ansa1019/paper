Visa is a global payments technology company that facilitates global commerce and money movement across more than 200 countries and territories through innovative technologies. We provide transaction processing services primarily through Visanet, our advanced transaction processing network, offering products and solutions that facilitate secure, reliable, and efficient money movement for all participants in the ecosystem. Our net revenues are primarily generated from payments volume on Visa products and the number of transactions processed on our network, with growth driven by nominal payments volume, processed transactions, and nominal cross-border volume. The increase in net revenues reflects our ability to leverage technology capabilities to enhance operational efficiency and respond to market dynamics. We have invested in technology assets and payments system infrastructure to support our strategic resource allocation and enhance our IT capabilities. Our acquisitions, including Currencycloud and Tink, are aimed at expanding our technology portfolio and enhancing our capabilities in providing innovative financial products and services. The strategic integration of technology resources is essential for transforming our business processes and enhancing our competitive advantage. Our operational flexibility is supported by effective management of our liquidity and capital resources, ensuring we can meet our business needs and respond to changing market conditions. We regularly evaluate cash requirements for current operations and may raise additional funds through debt or equity to support our technology investments. Our treasury policies guide the management of liquidity risk, ensuring we can complete payments settlement activities and make planned capital investments in technology. We maintain a commercial paper program to support working capital requirements, and our unsecured revolving credit facility provides additional liquidity. The management of our investment portfolio is designed to meet our working capital and liquidity needs while maximizing returns. Our ability to mobilize and deploy IT-based resources in combination with other capabilities is critical for achieving our organizational goals and enhancing our market responsiveness. The ongoing evaluation of our technology infrastructure and strategic management practices ensures that we remain agile in the face of environmental turbulence and competitive forces. Our focus on information management capability enables us to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, and security, which is vital for maintaining customer relationships and driving business value. The effective governance of our IT resources and capabilities is essential for supporting our organizational effectiveness and achieving performance excellence in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.
