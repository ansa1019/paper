The management of market risk is essential to achieving BB&T’s strategic financial objectives, with a focus on minimizing adverse effects from changes in market risk factors, including those related to technology deployment and operational flexibility. The company’s capital management processes ensure sufficient capital to absorb losses and maintain a highly capitalized position, which is crucial for instilling confidence in depositors and other funding sources. BB&T’s approach to risk management incorporates the evaluation of technology capabilities and their alignment with business strategies to optimize performance outcomes and enhance organizational effectiveness.
