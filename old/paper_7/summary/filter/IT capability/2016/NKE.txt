Nike's strategy is to achieve long-term revenue growth by creating innovative, "must have" products, building deep personal consumer connections with brands, and delivering compelling consumer experiences at retail and online. The company continues to strive for operational excellence by investing in new technologies that increase automation, help reduce waste, and have long-term potential to increase customization of products and speed to market. Investments in infrastructure improve the efficiency and effectiveness of operations, while investments in consumer engagement drive economic returns in the form of incremental revenue and gross profit. The growth in the higher-margin direct-to-consumer (DTC) business is a key focus, with DTC revenues representing approximately 26% of total Nike brand revenues. The DTC operations include Nike-owned in-line and factory stores, as well as online sales through Nike-owned websites. The company has achieved significant revenue growth driven by innovative performance products incorporating proprietary technology platforms, which reinforce brand connections with consumers through investments in endorsements, high-impact marketing, and digital marketing. The increase in revenues was fueled by strong demand for Nike brand products across nearly every geography and key category. The company's operational strategies are designed to deliver sustainable, profitable long-term growth through effective management of its diversified portfolio of businesses. The focus on digital capabilities is evident in the investments made to support the DTC business, including new store openings and enhancements to consumer-focused digital capabilities. The company's commitment to leveraging technology resources and capabilities is reflected in its approach to managing working capital efficiently and deploying capital effectively. The integration of technology into the supply chain is seen as a competitive advantage, enabling the company to respond to market dynamics and consumer demands more effectively. The emphasis on operational flexibility and the ability to tailor offerings in response to changing business needs underscores the importance of IT capability in supporting business strategies and enhancing work processes. The company's financial performance metrics indicate a strong return on invested capital and sustained revenue growth, which are attributed to the effective mobilization and deployment of IT-based resources in conjunction with other strategic resources. The focus on innovation partnerships and digital transformation highlights the company's commitment to enhancing its IT architecture and capabilities, ensuring that it remains competitive in a rapidly evolving market environment.
