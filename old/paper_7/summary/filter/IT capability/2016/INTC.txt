Our management's discussion and analysis of financial condition and results of operations highlights the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities. We are building on our strong position in client computing and are investing for growth in the data center, internet of things market segments, and disruptive differentiated memory technology. To accelerate our transformation, we announced the 2016 restructuring program, which is on track to reduce our headcount and generate savings, reallocating these savings to our growth segments, such as the data center and internet of things, while continuing to invest in areas that extend our leadership in Moore's law and expand market opportunities. The integration of Altera has been a key component of our strategy, as we have worked to enhance our IT capabilities through the acquisition of programmable semiconductors and related products, which support our operational efficiency and business transformation efforts. Our strategy for the notebook computing market segment focuses on offering technologies designed to improve performance, battery life, wireless connectivity, manageability, and security, which are essential for meeting evolving business needs. The data center group segment emphasizes lowering the total cost of ownership and optimizing specific workloads for enterprise and cloud service providers, reflecting our commitment to enhancing IT capabilities that drive competitive advantage. We have launched platforms with advancements designed for real-time analytics and in-memory computing, addressing emerging data analytics and artificial intelligence solutions, which are critical for maintaining market agility and responsiveness. Our investment in research and development has increased, driven by higher investment in strategically important areas such as servers and the internet of things, which reflects our focus on enhancing our technology portfolio and capabilities. The internet of things group segment aims to establish an end-to-end manageable architecture that captures actionable information from connected devices, further demonstrating our commitment to integrating IT capabilities across various market segments. We recognize the importance of information management capability, ensuring that we provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, and security, which are vital for supporting our organizational goals. The operational flexibility gained through our IT investments allows us to respond effectively to market dynamics and consumer demands. Our ongoing evaluation of technology resources and capabilities positions us to capitalize on investment opportunities and navigate regulatory contexts, thereby enhancing our organizational performance and sustaining our competitive positioning in the marketplace.
