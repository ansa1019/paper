The company continues to build upon its investments in technology, focusing on restaurant modernization and technology to transform the restaurant service experience and enhance the brand in the eyes of the customer. By evolving the technology platform, the company is expanding choices for how customers order, pay, and are served through additional functionality on its global mobile app, self-order kiosks, and technology-driven models that enable table service and curb-side pick-up. The modernization efforts are designed to drive incremental customer visits and higher average check. The company has met aggressive deployment targets for experience of the future, digital, and delivery accelerators, enhancing the overall customer experience with hospitable, friendly service and ever-improving convenience. The company is also enhancing financial value through its refranchising efforts, which provide a more predictable and stable revenue stream with significantly lower operating costs and risks. The transition to a more heavily franchised business model is benefiting the company's performance, as the rent and royalty income received from franchisees supports operational flexibility and resource management. The velocity growth plan outlines actions to drive sustainable guest count growth, a reliable long-term measure of the company's strength, that is vital to growing sales and shareholder value. The strategy is built on pillars focusing on retaining existing customers, regaining lost customers, and converting casual to committed customers, all of which are supported by technology capabilities that enhance customer relationships and operational efficiency. The company has established sustainable platforms that enable execution of the plan with greater speed, efficiency, and impact while remaining focused on the fundamentals of running great restaurants. The company’s capital expenditures are primarily allocated to reinvestment in existing restaurants and new restaurant openings, which are concentrated in markets with strong returns and opportunities for long-term growth. The company's ability to manage inflationary cost increases effectively is attributed to rapid inventory turnover, the ability to adjust menu prices, cost controls, and substantial property holdings. The company’s significant operations outside the U.S. contribute to its overall financial performance, with a substantial portion of earnings reinvested in foreign jurisdictions to support ongoing development and growth. The company expects to continue to leverage its technology investments to enhance customer experience and operational capabilities, which are critical for maintaining competitive positioning and achieving long-term sustainable growth. The company's focus on digital transformation and IT deployment is integral to its strategy for improving operational performance and aligning business processes with customer needs. The company’s strategic direction emphasizes the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities to support business strategies and enhance organizational effectiveness.
