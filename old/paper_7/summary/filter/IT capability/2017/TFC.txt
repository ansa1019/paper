BB&T has made substantial investments and incurred significant costs related to personnel, infrastructure, and other items in response to increased regulations, which reflects the challenges associated with IT projects. The company’s business is dynamic and complex, leading management to annually evaluate and adjust the business strategy in the context of the current operating environment, considering the financial condition and performance of the company and expectations for future economic activity. Key challenges likely to impact performance include intense competition within the financial services industry and the cost and risk associated with regulatory initiatives and IT projects. The management of BB&T emphasizes the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities to transform the firm into a competitive force. The company focuses on information management capability, which encompasses the ability to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access, tailoring these in response to changing business needs and directions. BB&T's risk management framework establishes an environment that enables the achievement of superior performance relative to peers and ensures that the company is viewed among the safest of banks while allowing operational freedom to act on opportunities. The company actively manages market risk associated with asset and liability portfolios, focusing on strategic pricing and management of appropriate maturity mixes of assets and liabilities to produce consistent net interest income during periods of changing interest rates. The effective management of market risk is essential to achieving BB&T's strategic financial objectives, with the primary objectives being to minimize adverse effects from changes in market risk factors on net interest income, net income, and capital. BB&T's cybersecurity strategy is integral to its risk management, addressing the increasing sophistication of cyber attacks and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements related to data privacy and systems availability. The company employs various internal training methods to encourage reporting of potential security risks and utilizes third-party services to assess and supplement its cybersecurity needs. BB&T's operational risk management framework includes oversight of risks arising from inadequate or failed internal processes, people, and systems, as well as external events. The company’s strategic resource allocation is guided by its risk appetite, ensuring that risks are understood and managed effectively. BB&T’s approach to technology management involves continuous monitoring and evaluation of its systems and processes to enhance operational efficiency and support business innovation, thereby contributing to the overall organizational effectiveness and performance.
