3M is investing in business transformation, defined as changes in processes and internal/external service delivery across the company to improve operational efficiency and productivity, while allowing 3M to serve customers with greater speed and efficiency. This transformation is enabled by the ongoing multi-year phased implementation of an enterprise resource planning (ERP) system on a worldwide basis. The company is committed to leadership in sustainability and to enabling the advancement of energy solutions into the future, integrating its former renewable energy division into existing divisions within the electronics and energy business segment and safety and graphics business segment. 3M's investments in growth initiatives and optimization of its portfolio and supply chain footprint reflect its focus on opportunities that create greater value for its shareholders. The company is also actively considering additional acquisitions, investments, and strategic alliances to strengthen its portfolio. In 2017, 3M incurred significant strategic investments, primarily reported within the cost of sales and selling, general and administrative expenses, aimed at enhancing its operational capabilities. The company’s strategic investments included $413 million in incremental actions, with $96 million related to restructuring actions and $47 million to exit activities. 3M's capital spending is directed towards investments in property, plant, and equipment, which enable growth across diverse markets and increase manufacturing efficiency. The company expects to continue investing in its operations to drive growth, including continual review of acquisition opportunities. 3M's focus on research and development has resulted in a portion of its value being comprised of internally developed businesses, emphasizing the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities. The company is enhancing its IT capabilities to support business needs and improve information management, ensuring the provision of data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access. 3M's operational flexibility is further supported by its strategic resource allocation and investments in technology infrastructure, which facilitate process optimization and enhance organizational effectiveness. The company’s ability to manage its operations effectively is reflected in its strong cash flow generation and access to capital markets, which positions it well to address future challenges and opportunities in a dynamic business environment. 3M's ongoing commitment to innovation and technology management underpins its competitive advantage and market positioning, ensuring that it remains responsive to evolving consumer demands and market dynamics. The integration of technology capabilities across its business segments is critical for sustaining performance gains and achieving strategic objectives in the context of global competition and regulatory changes.
