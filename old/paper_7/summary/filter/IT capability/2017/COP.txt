We believe that our ability to control operating and overhead costs impacts our capacity to deliver strong cash from operations, which is critical in maintaining a competitive position in the industry. The energy industry has periodically experienced extreme volatility due to fluctuating supply-and-demand conditions, and our strategy is to create value through price cycles by delivering on disciplined financial and operational priorities. Our focus on financial returns is a core aspect of our value proposition, and we aim to achieve strong financial returns by controlling costs and exercising capital discipline. Our operational flexibility and strategic resource allocation are essential in responding to market dynamics and ensuring organizational effectiveness. Our ability to manage operating and overhead costs is critical to maintaining our competitive position, particularly in a low commodity price environment.
