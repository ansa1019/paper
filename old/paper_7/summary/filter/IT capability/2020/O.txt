The company is structured as a real estate investment trust (REIT), requiring it annually to distribute at least 90% of its taxable income in the form of dividends to its stockholders. The company has been acquiring and managing freestanding commercial properties that generate rental revenue under long-term net lease agreements with commercial clients. We strive to build mutually beneficial relationships with our clients, believing their success is our success. The company has a conservative capital structure, seeking to maintain a conservative debt level on its balance sheet and solid interest and fixed charge coverage ratios. We expect to fund our operating expenses and other short-term liquidity requirements primarily through cash provided by operating activities, borrowing on our credit facility, and through public securities offerings. Our investment-grade credit ratings provide for financing at favorable rates, and we continually monitor the commercial real estate and global credit markets to adjust our business strategy accordingly. We have established a $3.0 billion unsecured revolving credit facility and a commercial paper program to manage our liquidity needs. The company evaluates its capital investments and financing strategies to ensure alignment with business objectives and market conditions. We assess collectability based on creditworthiness and economic trends, particularly in light of the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic on our clients' industries. Our strategy includes acquiring properties that meet our investment criteria while maintaining operational flexibility and responsiveness to market dynamics. We capitalize costs on existing properties, which include re-leasing costs and building improvements, to enhance our portfolio's performance. Our financial covenants require us to maintain certain ratios, reflecting our ability to manage debt and ensure compliance with our credit agreements. We define adjusted EBITDA as a meaningful measure of our performance, providing insights into our operational efficiency and capacity to generate cash flow. The company's operational strategies are designed to leverage technology and information management capabilities to support business processes and enhance decision-making. We believe that maintaining a robust IT infrastructure is essential for optimizing our operational effectiveness and achieving strategic objectives. The company continues to evaluate its technology deployment to ensure that it aligns with evolving business needs and enhances our competitive positioning in the market. We recognize the importance of integrating technology capabilities into our business processes to drive innovation and improve performance outcomes. Our focus on strategic resource allocation and investment decisions reflects our commitment to maximizing shareholder value while navigating the complexities of the real estate market.
