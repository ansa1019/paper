The company operates through two reporting segments: interconnect products and assemblies and cable products and solutions, with a focus on information technology and communication devices and systems for the converging technologies of voice, video, and data communications. The company competes primarily on the basis of technology innovation, product quality, price, customer service, and delivery time, emphasizing the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with other resources and capabilities to support business needs. The company's strategy includes providing customers with comprehensive design capabilities and a broad selection of products while maintaining programs for productivity improvement and cost control. The company believes that its global presence is an important competitive advantage, allowing it to provide quality products on a timely basis to its multinational customers. In 2018, net sales to the information technology and data communications market increased, reflecting organic growth in products for data centers, including server and networking-related applications, storage, and consumer electronics. The company has focused its research and development efforts through close collaboration with its customers to develop highly-engineered products that meet customer needs and have the potential for broad market applications. The sales growth was driven by growth in the mobile devices, industrial, automotive, information technology and data communications, military, mobile networks, and commercial aerospace markets, indicating a strong alignment with market dynamics and consumer demands. The company’s strategic objective is to enhance its position in served markets by pursuing performance-enhancing interconnect solutions and fostering collaborative management. The company’s ability to generate cash from operations is a fundamental financial strength, with cash flow provided by operating activities reflecting an increase due to higher net income, which is essential for funding capital expenditures, acquisitions, and other operational needs. The company’s investment in modern manufacturing technologies and expansion into lower-cost areas further supports its operational flexibility and market agility. The company has also recognized the need for effective information management capabilities to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access, tailoring these in response to changing business needs. The company’s focus on controlling costs and pursuing strategic acquisitions and investments is integral to its overall business strategy, which aims to leverage technology capabilities to transform the firm into a competitive force. The increase in operating income margin for the interconnect products and assemblies segment was driven primarily by strong operating leverage on higher sales volumes, highlighting the importance of operational efficiency and effective resource deployment. The company’s strategic management practices and technology infrastructure are designed to enhance organizational effectiveness and support ongoing business transformation initiatives.
