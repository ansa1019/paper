The company employs rigorous training and audit programs to drive ongoing improvement in both personal and process safety. The optimization of utilization rates at its refineries through reliable and safe operations enables the capture of market value in terms of prices and margins. The company has budgeted significant capital expenditures and investments aimed at growth, particularly in its midstream and chemicals segments, which are expected to benefit from cost-advantaged feedstocks and infrastructure-driven capital investment opportunities. Phillips 66 partners with various entities to enhance its operational capabilities, including the formation of Phillips 66 Partners, a master limited partnership designed to fund midstream growth efficiently. The company focuses on funding the most attractive growth opportunities across its portfolio while ensuring disciplined capital allocation to projects that generate competitive returns. The company’s operational flexibility and strategic resource allocation are critical in navigating market dynamics, particularly in a low commodity price environment.
