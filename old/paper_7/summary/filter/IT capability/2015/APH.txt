The company operates through two reporting segments: interconnect products and assemblies and cable products and solutions, with a focus on information technology and communication devices and systems for converging technologies of voice, video, and data communications. The company encounters competition primarily based on technology innovation, product quality, price, customer service, and delivery time, emphasizing the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities. The company has positioned itself to compete effectively by focusing its global resources, believing its global presence is a competitive advantage that allows for timely product delivery to multinational customers. The strategy includes providing comprehensive design capabilities and a broad selection of products while maintaining productivity improvements and cost control. Research and development efforts are closely aligned with OEM customers to develop highly-engineered products that meet customer needs, reflecting the company's commitment to innovation and market responsiveness. In 2015, sales growth was driven by increases in automotive, mobile devices, industrial, and information technology markets, with significant contributions from both organic growth and acquisitions. The company reported increases in sales to the information technology and data communications market, primarily due to growth in products for server, web, and data center applications. The company also focuses on controlling costs through investments in modern manufacturing technologies and expanding into lower-cost areas. The strategic objective is to enhance its market position by developing performance-enhancing interconnect solutions, controlling costs, pursuing strategic acquisitions, and fostering collaborative management. The ability to generate cash from operations is highlighted as a fundamental financial strength, providing flexibility to meet financial commitments and fund capital expenditures, acquisitions, and dividends. The company utilizes debt financing to lower the overall cost of capital and increase return on stockholders' equity, with a history of borrowing domestically at reasonable interest rates. The company’s cash flow provided by operating activities has increased, reflecting net income growth and improved working capital management. The company has made significant investments in technology and infrastructure to support its operational efficiency and business transformation initiatives, ensuring alignment with strategic goals and market demands. The focus on IT capability is evident in the company's commitment to enhancing its operational flexibility, resource allocation, and overall organizational effectiveness, which are critical for maintaining competitive advantage in a rapidly evolving market landscape. The company’s strategic management practices emphasize the integration of technology capabilities to optimize business processes and improve performance metrics, ensuring that it remains responsive to external influences and market dynamics.
