The company’s management believes that tailored branding, packaging, pricing, and distribution channel strategies help achieve profitable growth. The company is committed to meeting changing consumer needs through beverage innovation, choice, and variety, and it continuously strives to maintain profit margins. The company recognizes that consumer discretionary spending represents a challenge to the successful marketing and sale of its products. The company’s future success will depend, in part, upon its ability to penetrate new domestic and international markets and to gain approval for the sale of products in various countries.
