Phillips 66 is committed to optimizing utilization rates at its refineries through reliable and safe operations, which enables the company to capture market value in terms of prices and margins. The company focuses on maintaining strong operating excellence, which includes continuous improvement in safety, environmental stewardship, reliability, and cost efficiency. It employs rigorous training and audit programs to drive ongoing improvement in both personal and process safety, striving for zero incidents. The company has budgeted $4.6 billion in capital expenditures and investments for 2015, with a total capital program expected to be $6.7 billion, aimed primarily at growing its midstream and chemicals segments. This growth is driven by the need for additional infrastructure to support domestic unconventional crude oil and natural gas production, creating capital investment opportunities. Phillips 66 emphasizes a disciplined capital allocation process to ensure investments focus on projects that generate competitive returns throughout the business cycle. The company also aims to enhance returns through greater use of advantaged feedstocks and portfolio optimization, which is crucial for driving higher returns in its marketing and specialties segment. The organization strives to attract, train, develop, and retain individuals with the necessary knowledge and skills to implement its business strategy, supporting its values and ethics. This focus on building a high-performing organization is essential for achieving operational efficiency and organizational effectiveness. The company’s strategic priorities include delivering profitable growth, enhancing returns, and growing shareholder distributions, which are believed to enhance shareholder value through consistent and ongoing growth of regular dividends, supplemented by share repurchases. Phillips 66 has formed Phillips 66 Partners, a master limited partnership, to own, operate, develop, and acquire primarily fee-based crude oil, refined petroleum product, and natural gas liquids pipelines and terminals, which are integral to its operational capabilities. The company’s capital expenditures and investments in information technology and facilities are aimed at supporting its overall business strategy and enhancing its operational capabilities. The organization is also focused on funding the most attractive growth opportunities across its portfolio while maintaining strong operating excellence and ensuring that its investments align with business needs and market dynamics. The company’s operational flexibility and strategic resource allocation are crucial for responding to market changes and achieving its organizational goals.
