We are committed to maintaining and continuously improving our lean cost structure through detailed attention to the cost of each of the products that we offer and our organizational structure, with a focus on reducing the cost of each. This commitment reflects our approach to operational flexibility and resource allocation, which are critical components of organizational resilience. By optimizing our cost structure, we aim to enhance our ability to absorb strain and preserve functioning despite external pressures and challenges. Our focus on continuous improvement aligns with the principles of maintaining positive adjustment under challenging conditions, as we strive to confront and benefit from sudden disruptive changes. The emphasis on a lean cost structure also supports our strategic initiatives aimed at enhancing organizational health and effectiveness, thereby contributing to our overall resilience. Through these efforts, we seek to ensure that our organization remains adaptable and capable of responding effectively to environmental disruptions, thereby maintaining our performance and integrity in the face of adversity.
