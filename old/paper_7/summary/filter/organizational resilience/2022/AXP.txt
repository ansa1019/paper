We are a globally integrated payments company with a range of products and services offered globally to diverse customer groups, including consumers, small businesses, mid-sized companies, and large corporations. Our investments in product innovation, technology, people, and our brand have led to increased generational relevance with millennial and Gen Z customers, record new card acquisitions, deeper relationships with customers, and expanded merchant acceptance. Our performance continues to give us confidence in our business model and strategy, and while we recognize the uncertainty of the geopolitical and macroeconomic environment, we remain focused on delivering sustainable and profitable growth. We have made significant investments in marketing to drive growth momentum and accelerate new card acquisitions. Our comprehensive enterprise-wide risk management (ERM) program is designed to identify, aggregate, monitor, measure, report, and manage risks, defining our risk appetite, governance, culture, and capabilities. The implementation and execution of the ERM program is overseen by our chief risk officer and monitored by our board of directors through various committees. Our risk management framework includes operational risk management, which is critical to maintaining organizational resilience, as it addresses the risk of loss due to inadequate or failed processes, people, or information systems. We also focus on information and cyber security to protect our systems from unauthorized access and disruptions. Our liquidity management strategy aims to maintain access to diverse funding sources to meet our business requirements and expected future financing obligations, especially during economic downturns. We seek to maintain a balanced debt maturity profile and a strong liquidity position to support our operations, which is essential for organizational survival and adaptability. Our funding strategy is designed to ensure that we can continuously meet our obligations and manage risks effectively. We recognize the importance of maintaining operational flexibility and resilience in the face of external pressures, including economic, geopolitical, and competitive conditions. Our approach to risk management includes assessing the implications of market volatility and environmental disruptions on our operations and financial performance. We are committed to enhancing our capabilities in crisis management and emergency preparedness to ensure business continuity and recovery from unexpected situations. The strength of our organizational practices and our ability to adapt to changing circumstances are vital for maintaining performance under pressure and achieving our major objectives. We continuously evaluate our strategies to ensure that we can confront challenges and leverage opportunities for growth and innovation. Our focus on organizational health and effectiveness is reflected in our commitment to resource management, strategic planning, and stakeholder engagement, which are essential for fostering resilience and ensuring long-term success in a turbulent market environment.
