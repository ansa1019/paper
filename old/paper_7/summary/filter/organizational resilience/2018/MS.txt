The firm employs various risk mitigation strategies, including diversification of risk exposures and hedging, to manage market risk associated with its market-making activities. The firm actively manages its secured financings to reduce potential refinancing risk of secured financings of less liquid assets. The firm considers the quality of collateral when negotiating collateral eligibility with counterparties and utilizes shorter-term secured financing for highly liquid assets. The firm has established procedures to mitigate the risk that a counterparty's performance obligations will be unenforceable, including consideration of counterparty legal authority and capacity. The firm maintains methodologies for calculating an allowance for loan losses that estimates the inherent losses in the loan portfolio. The allowance for loan losses is maintained at a level to ensure that it is reasonably likely to adequately absorb the estimated probable losses inherent in the portfolio.
