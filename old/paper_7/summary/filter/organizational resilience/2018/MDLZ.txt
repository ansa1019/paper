Non-GAAP adjustments related to the simplify to grow program reflect costs incurred that relate to the objectives of our program to transform our supply chain network and organizational structure. The program aims to enhance organizational resilience by addressing internal knowledge and resource allocation to adapt to environmental pressures and disruptions. Costs that do not meet the program objectives are not reflected in the non-GAAP adjustments, indicating a strategic response to ensure operational continuity and performance maintenance under challenging conditions. This transformation is essential for the organization to confront unexpected situations and maintain its integrity and performance amidst external disturbances. The focus on reconfiguring the organizational structure is indicative of a broader strategy to improve adaptability and resource generation, which are critical components of organizational resilience. The initiative underscores the importance of aligning operational resources with major objectives to foster a resilient business environment capable of thriving in the face of adversity.
