We are a leading global provider of financial services technology, providing account processing systems, electronic payments processing products and services, and related services necessary for our clients to operate their businesses. Our operations are comprised of the payments segment and the financial institution services segment, which support numerous types of financial transactions. We frequently review our portfolio to ensure we have the right set of businesses to execute our strategy, expecting to acquire businesses that meet client demand or change industry dynamics. Our strategic initiatives include enhancing the overall value of our existing client relationships, improving operational effectiveness, and differentiating our products and services through innovation. The evolving regulatory and cybersecurity landscape creates a challenging operating environment, driving interest in solutions that help financial institutions win and retain customers, generate revenue, and enhance operating efficiency. We anticipate that financial institutions will continue to invest in managing information and offering innovative services in a rapidly evolving environment. Our focus on long-term client relationships and recurring, transaction-oriented products has reduced the impact of consolidation in the financial services industry. We believe that our sizable and diverse client base, combined with our position as a leading provider of non-discretionary, recurring revenue-based products, provides a solid foundation for growth. Our enterprise priorities for 2019 include building high-quality revenue while meeting earnings goals, enhancing client relationships with an emphasis on digital solutions, and delivering innovation that enables differentiated value for our clients. Our revenue is diversified, and we have clients spanning the entire range of financial institutions, which mitigates risks associated with client loss due to mergers or acquisitions. The integration of our products and services creates a compelling value proposition for our clients, providing new sources of revenue and opportunities to reduce costs. We continue to implement strategic initiatives to move money and information effectively, which is essential for maintaining operational continuity and resilience in the face of market changes. Our operational management focuses on resource allocation and strategic adaptation to ensure we can respond effectively to external pressures and environmental disruptions. The market for products and services offered by financial institutions continues to evolve, and we expect to benefit from the trend of financial institutions moving to outsourced solutions as they seek to remain current with technology changes. We have established mechanisms for risk management and crisis response to enhance our organizational resilience, allowing us to maintain performance under pressure and adapt to complex situations. Our ability to foresee and confront challenges, along with our commitment to innovation and operational flexibility, positions us to thrive in turbulent markets and achieve our major objectives.
