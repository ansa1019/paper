We believe our integrated business model strengthens our route-to-market and provides opportunities for net sales and profit growth through the alignment of the economic interests of our brand ownership and our manufacturing and distribution businesses. The beverage market is subject to some seasonal variations, and our beverage sales are generally higher during the warmer months and can be influenced by the timing of holidays as well as weather fluctuations. We are impacted by shifting consumer demographics and needs, and we believe marketing and product innovations that target fast-growing population segments could drive market growth.
