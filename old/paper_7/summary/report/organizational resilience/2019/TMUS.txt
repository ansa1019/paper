The objectives of our management's discussion and analysis of financial condition and results of operations are to provide users of our consolidated financial statements with a narrative explanation from the perspective of management of our financial condition, results of operations, cash flows, liquidity, and certain other factors that may affect future results. Our un-carrier strategy has disrupted the wireless communications services industry by actively engaging with and listening to our customers, eliminating their existing pain points, and providing them with an exceptional experience. The merger with Sprint is expected to enhance our capabilities to respond effectively to environmental disruptions and transform ourselves to emerge stronger from challenging situations. Our ability to acquire and retain branded customers is crucial for revenue generation, and we believe our un-carrier strategy, along with ongoing network improvements, has been successful in attracting and retaining customers. Total revenues increased, driven by growth in service revenues, which reflects our adaptability to changing market conditions. The positive impact of hurricane-related reimbursements in prior years highlights our capacity for recovery from unexpected situations. Our operating expenses have increased primarily from higher selling, general, and administrative expenses, which indicates our commitment to maintaining operational flexibility and resilience in the face of rising costs. The adoption of the new lease standard has positively impacted our financial performance, demonstrating our ability to reconfigure resources effectively. The increase in net cash provided by operating activities signifies our operational continuity and resilience in generating cash flow. We maintain a focus on strategic initiatives that enhance our organizational health and competitive advantage, including network expansion and the deployment of our 5G network. Our financial condition remains robust, with significant liquidity available to support future investments and operational needs. The increase in our customer base and the reduction in churn rates reflect our successful adaptation strategies and customer engagement efforts. We continuously assess our operational resources and capabilities to ensure we are well-positioned to confront external pressures and market volatility. The strategic management of our resources, including capital expenditures for network infrastructure, is critical for sustaining our performance under pressure. Our commitment to innovation and service excellence is evident in our ongoing efforts to enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty, which are essential for our long-term success. The anticipated merger with Sprint is expected to create synergies that will further strengthen our organizational resilience and ability to navigate complex situations. We are prepared to leverage our organizational intelligence and dynamic capabilities to address future challenges and capitalize on opportunities for growth. Our performance maintenance strategies are designed to ensure we remain agile and responsive to the evolving business environment, ultimately supporting our goal of flourishing in a competitive landscape.
