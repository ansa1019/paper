Management has continued to prioritize investments in solutions that help address the needs of clients in order to increase the company's potential to resume strong revenue growth following the pandemic. In response to COVID-19, several actions have been taken to manage discretionary expenses, including reducing office space, prohibiting most travel, and accelerating automation and functional alignment across the organization. The company has implemented a comprehensive pandemic plan that includes site-specific plans, travel restrictions, medical response protocols, work-from-home strategies, and enhanced cleaning within locations to maintain business continuity. Nearly 95% of employees have remained in a work-from-home status and have been effectively outfitted to continue providing necessary services to clients. The ongoing monitoring of the effects of the pandemic has led to modifications in business practices, ensuring operational flexibility and resilience in the face of external pressures. The company has also expanded sick leave for employees affected by COVID-19 and provided special pay for employees involved in critical infrastructure who could not work from home, demonstrating a commitment to organizational health and employee support systems. The shift in consumer preference from traditional branch banking services to digital banking solutions has accelerated the digitization of banking and payment services, requiring the organization to adapt and innovate in response to changing customer needs. Investments in modernization, innovation, and integrated solutions are aimed at enhancing capabilities and addressing emerging technology trends. The company's strategic initiatives include the development of a modern banking platform that is among the first cloud-native core banking solutions, reflecting a proactive approach to resilience and adaptability. The integration of acquired companies, such as Worldpay, has been a focus to achieve potential incremental revenue opportunities and expense efficiencies, with a history of successfully integrating operations and technology platforms. The company has developed fraud, security, risk management, and compliance solutions to target growth opportunities in the financial services industry, emphasizing the importance of strategic resilience and risk mitigation. The liquidity position has improved throughout the year, with a commitment to reducing leverage while ensuring ample liquidity, which is critical for organizational survival during turbulent market conditions. The ongoing evolution of the company's operational management practices and strategic planning reflects a multifaceted approach to maintaining performance under pressure and navigating complex situations. The focus on collaborative networks and inter-organizational collaboration enhances the company's ability to respond effectively to environmental disruptions and to leverage its strengths in the competitive landscape. Overall, the company's actions and strategies underscore its commitment to organizational resilience, ensuring that it can absorb strain and preserve functioning despite adversity.
