Our actionable solutions power better investment decisions by enabling our clients to understand and analyze key drivers of risk and return, thereby enhancing operational flexibility and adaptability in response to changing investment trends and an increasingly complex industry. Our growth strategy includes extending leadership in research-enhanced content across asset classes, enhancing distribution and content-enabling technology, expanding solutions that empower client customization, and executing strategic relationships and acquisitions with complementary content and technology companies. As of December 31, 2020, we had offices in more than 30 cities across more than 20 countries, which supports our ability to manage and control the growth of our operations in diverse markets. Our focus on product innovation addresses the evolving needs of our clients, which is critical for maintaining competitive advantage under external pressures and environmental disruptions. We utilize a portfolio of key financial metrics to manage the company, including operating metrics such as run rate, subscription sales, and retention rate, which provide insight into our performance under pressure and our ability to adapt to unexpected situations. Our retention rate is an important metric as it reflects our organizational capabilities to maintain client relationships and respond effectively to subscription cancellations, which can impact our revenue and operational continuity. The total run rate grew 12.1% to $1,832.5 million as of December 31, 2020, driven by strong growth in recurring subscriptions and asset-based fees, indicating our resilience in navigating market volatility. We believe that our liquidity, along with other financing alternatives, will provide the necessary capital to fund strategic initiatives and achieve our planned growth, reinforcing our organizational resilience. Our financial performance is characterized by a focus on revenue and profit growth, with adjusted EBITDA increasing 14.2% to $971.5 million for the year ended December 31, 2020, reflecting our ability to absorb strain and preserve functioning despite the presence of adversity. Our operational management practices emphasize resource allocation and effective decision-making, which are essential for maintaining organizational health and ensuring performance maintenance during turbulent market conditions. We continuously evaluate acquisition and strategic partnership opportunities to enhance our operational capabilities and foster inter-organizational collaboration, which is vital for thriving in a complex and rapidly changing business environment.
