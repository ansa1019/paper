The company’s ability to absorb strain and preserve functioning despite adversities is reflected in its strong financial performance, with sales of $8.9 billion in fiscal year 2018, representing a 9% increase over the prior year. This growth was primarily driven by higher volumes from base business growth and new industrial gas project onstreams, showcasing the company's operational flexibility and resource allocation capabilities. The execution of gasification strategies, including significant projects in Saudi Arabia and China, illustrates the company's proactive measures to reconfigure its operations in response to market demands and environmental perturbations. The formation of joint ventures and strategic partnerships further enhances Air Products' competitive advantage and resilience strategies, allowing for effective resource generation and inter-organizational collaboration. The company’s strong financial position, with assets of $19.2 billion and a workforce of approximately 16,300 employees, supports its capacity to confront unexpected situations and maintain performance under pressure. The adjusted EBITDA margin of 34.9% indicates effective management of operational resources and the ability to sustain performance amidst challenging conditions. The company's commitment to innovation and service development is evidenced by its ongoing investments in research and development, which are crucial for fostering organizational learning and adaptability. The emphasis on safety and operational excellence contributes to organizational health and the maintenance of positive adjustment under challenging conditions.
