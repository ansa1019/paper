Management actively monitors macroeconomic factors and their implications on the company's financial condition, liquidity, and operations, acknowledging that continued or renewed disruptions could negatively impact business performance. The company’s ability to respond effectively to environmental disruptions is critical for maintaining operational continuity and achieving strategic objectives. The organization aims to leverage its strengths in operational flexibility and resource allocation to navigate complex situations and external pressures.
