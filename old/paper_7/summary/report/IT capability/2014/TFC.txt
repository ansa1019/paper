BB&T's business has become more dynamic and complex, leading management to annually evaluate and adjust the company's business strategy in the context of the current operating environment. This includes considering the current financial condition and performance of the company, as well as expectations for future economic activity from both national and local market perspectives. The challenges relevant to BB&T's performance include intense competition within the financial services industry, global economic and geopolitical risks, and the costs and risks associated with regulatory reform and IT projects. Management emphasizes the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities to transform the firm into a competitive force. The company has invested in strategic mergers to complement organic growth, which enhances its operational flexibility and market responsiveness. Furthermore, BB&T's risk management framework establishes an environment to achieve superior performance relative to peers, ensuring that the bank is viewed among the safest of banks and preserving asset value. The effective management of market risk is essential to achieving BB&T's strategic financial objectives, particularly regarding interest rate risk in its balance sheet. BB&T actively manages market risk associated with asset and liability portfolios, focusing on the strategic pricing of asset and liability accounts and the management of appropriate maturity mixes. The company's technology infrastructure supports its operations, facilitating the management of risks and enhancing decision-making capabilities. BB&T utilizes various internal training methods to promote cybersecurity awareness among employees, recognizing the increasing sophistication of cyber threats. The company has established a risk management framework based on a "three lines of defense" model, ensuring effective oversight and guidance of risk-taking across the enterprise. The management of credit risk is critical, with BB&T employing strict underwriting criteria and continuous monitoring of individual loans and lending relationships. The company also utilizes automated credit scoring to assist with underwriting credit risk in its sales finance portfolio. BB&T's liquidity management is robust, with a focus on maintaining a liquid asset buffer to meet funding needs and ensuring access to various funding sources. The bank's capital management processes are designed to maintain sufficient capital to absorb losses and instill confidence in depositors. BB&T's commitment to technology management and innovation is evident in its ongoing investments in IT capabilities, which are integral to supporting business strategies and enhancing operational efficiency. The company recognizes the importance of aligning its technology resources with business needs to optimize performance and achieve long-term financial goals.
