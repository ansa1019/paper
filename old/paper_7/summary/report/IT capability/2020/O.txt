Realty Income has been acquiring and managing freestanding commercial properties that generate rental revenue under long-term net lease agreements with our commercial clients. We strive to build mutually beneficial relationships with our clients, as their success is integral to our own. Our capital philosophy emphasizes a conservative capital structure, maintaining a conservative debt level on our balance sheet and solid interest and fixed charge coverage ratios. We expect to fund our operating expenses and other short-term liquidity requirements primarily through cash provided by operating activities, borrowings on our credit facility, and public securities offerings. The company continually monitors the commercial real estate and global credit markets and will adjust our business strategy as required. Our investment strategy includes issuing common stock when share prices allow for accretive investments into additional properties and utilizing proceeds for property acquisitions and general corporate purposes. We have established a $3.0 billion unsecured revolving credit facility to support liquidity needs and property acquisitions. Our credit facility provides for financing at competitive rates based on our investment-grade credit ratings. We have a diversified portfolio of properties across various industries, with a high occupancy rate, indicating effective management of our assets. The company has recorded provisions for impairment due to the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on certain clients, which has necessitated a reassessment of our asset values. We have also implemented measures to manage operational efficiency and maintain our competitive positioning in the market. Our focus on information management capability is critical, as we aim to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy and timeliness, which supports our business processes and strategic resource allocation. The ongoing evaluation of our operational capabilities and management skills is essential for adapting to market dynamics and ensuring organizational effectiveness. We recognize the importance of leveraging technology resources and capabilities to enhance our operational flexibility and responsiveness to market changes. Our strategy includes monitoring and optimizing our investment decisions to align with business needs and performance outcomes. The integration of technology into our business processes is vital for maintaining competitive advantage and supporting our long-term growth objectives.
