Duke Energy has continued to leverage new technology, digital tools, and data analytics across the business in response to a transforming landscape. The company recognizes the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities. In 2021, Duke Energy implemented the first three jurisdictional releases of Customer Connect, a new system that consolidates four legacy billing systems into one customer-service platform, allowing for a universal experience customers expect. The modernization of the electric grid, including smart meters and self-healing technologies, has improved system reliability and flexibility, providing better information and services for customers. Additionally, the company has partnered with Accenture and Microsoft to develop a novel technology platform aimed at measuring baseline methane emissions from natural gas distribution systems with a high level of accuracy in near real time. Duke Energy's commitment to digital transformation is evident in its strategic focus on deploying digital tools across its business and enhancing operational efficiency. The company has made significant investments in grid improvement and clean energy, which are key components of its strategy to reduce carbon emissions and enhance customer experience. The integration of advanced technologies and data management practices is expected to facilitate operational flexibility and improve decision-making speed, thereby enhancing overall firm performance. The strategic management practices employed by Duke Energy underscore the importance of information management capability, which includes providing data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, and security. The company's focus on technology deployment and infrastructure capability is critical for achieving its long-term financial goals and maintaining competitive advantage in a dynamic market environment. Furthermore, the establishment of a framework for stakeholder involvement in energy policy objectives reflects the company's commitment to strategic resource allocation and governance contexts that align with its clean energy transformation goals. Duke Energy's ongoing efforts to modernize its operations and enhance its IT capabilities are integral to its business strategy, aimed at delivering reliable and sustainable energy solutions while responding to evolving market demands and regulatory requirements.
