Simon Property Group, Inc. operates as a self-administered and self-managed real estate investment trust, focusing on maximizing total financial return through the enhancement of profitability and operation of its properties and investments. The company seeks growth in earnings and cash flows by attracting high-quality tenants and utilizing economies of scale to reduce operating expenses. It aims to enhance the profitability and market share of existing assets through selective acquisitions and redevelopment projects, which include the addition of anchors and big box tenants. The firm’s strategy includes establishing its malls as leading market resource providers for retailers and consumer-focused corporate alliances, which involves leveraging technology functionalities and digital technologies to generate consumer traffic. Simon Property Group evaluates acquisition opportunities based on their ability to enhance its portfolio, indicating a focus on strategic resource allocation and operational flexibility. The company employs a three-fold capital strategy to provide necessary capital for growth, maintain flexibility to access various forms of capital, and manage its financial structure to preserve investment-grade credit ratings. The impact of the COVID-19 pandemic has necessitated changes in operational practices, including cost reduction measures and temporary furloughs, highlighting the importance of IT capabilities in responding to market dynamics and external influences. The firm’s IT capabilities are critical in mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources, which support business needs and enhance organizational effectiveness. The company’s focus on information management capability ensures the provision of accurate, timely, and reliable data to users, facilitating informed decision-making and strategic alignment with business objectives. Simon Property Group’s investment in technology infrastructure and digital transformation initiatives reflects its commitment to enhancing operational efficiency and maintaining competitive advantage in the retail real estate sector. The firm’s ability to adapt to changing business environments and consumer demands underscores the significance of its IT governance and resource management practices. The company’s strategic management practices, including process optimization and technology integration, are essential for achieving performance gains and sustaining organizational performance amidst evolving market conditions.
