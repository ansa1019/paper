We are a leading provider of transparent and independent ratings, benchmarks, analytics, and data to the capital and commodity markets worldwide. Our operations consist of three reportable segments: ratings, market and commodities intelligence, and S&P Dow Jones indices. Our strategy includes achieving financial targets and creating shareholder value by focusing on organic revenue growth and continuing to deliver margin expansion with a focus on operating leverage and efficiency opportunities. We aim to deliver greater customer value through deeper client and market insights, innovative solutions, stronger internal teamwork, and reliable, nimble go-to-market processes. Enhancing planning and software engineering processes to speed up the delivery of content and products is a priority, as is applying lean management, robotics, automation, and machine learning to streamline internal workflow and deliver productivity. Strengthening our digital infrastructure capabilities, with an emphasis on workplace services and cybersecurity, is also essential. Our commitment to a disciplined and practical risk, control, and compliance environment supports our operational excellence and continuous innovation. We strive for a performance culture to drive innovation, flexibility, and agility to address customer needs, committing to leadership development programs and skills training while enhancing technology talent and skills across the company. Our market and commodities intelligence segment's portfolio of capabilities is designed to help the financial community, corporations, and professional service firms track performance, generate better investment returns, identify new trading and investment ideas, perform risk analysis, and develop mitigation strategies. We have integrated and leveraged recent acquisitions to develop and expand our analytical capabilities and offerings. The segment also launched a beta version of the new market intelligence platform in 2017. Our focus on operational efficiency and technology management is reflected in our investments in infrastructure and technology projects, which are critical for enhancing our service delivery and maintaining competitive positioning in the market. We continuously evaluate our technology resources and capabilities to ensure alignment with our strategic objectives and to support our business transformation efforts. Our commitment to improving information management capability underlines our ability to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access. This capability is vital for mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities. The integration of technology into our business processes is essential for enhancing operational flexibility and responding to market dynamics. Our governance frameworks ensure that we effectively manage our IT investments and align them with our organizational goals, thereby enhancing our overall performance and competitive advantage in the marketplace.
