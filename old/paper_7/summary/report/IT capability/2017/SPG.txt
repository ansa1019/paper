Simon Property Group, Inc. operates as a self-administered and self-managed real estate investment trust, focusing on maximizing total financial return through the enhancement of profitability and operation of its properties and investments. The company seeks growth in earnings and cash flows by attracting and retaining high-quality tenants, utilizing economies of scale, expanding and re-tenanting existing locations, selectively acquiring high-quality real estate assets, and generating consumer traffic through marketing initiatives and strategic corporate alliances. The organization emphasizes the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities to transform the firm into a competitive force. This includes enhancing its information management capability to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access, tailored to respond to changing business needs and directions. The firm’s IT capabilities represent the application of physical or intangible IT resources such as technology, knowledge, practices, relationships, management skills, and business process understanding to further organizational goals. The company routinely reviews and evaluates acquisition opportunities based on their ability to enhance its portfolio, reflecting a strategic resource allocation approach that supports operational flexibility and market responsiveness. Simon Property Group's international strategy involves partnering with established real estate companies and financing international investments with local currency to minimize foreign exchange risk, demonstrating an understanding of the external influences on its operations. The firm’s financing strategy relies primarily on long-term fixed-rate debt, with a focus on maintaining sufficient flexibility to access capital in various forms to support growth initiatives. The organization also engages in redevelopment and expansion projects to enhance the profitability and market share of existing assets, indicating a commitment to process optimization and innovation. The company’s ability to generate positive cash flow from operations is essential for meeting operating expenses and capital expenditures, while its investment decisions are guided by a risk-reward criteria that align with its strategic objectives. Additionally, Simon Property Group recognizes the significance of technology deployment and integration in driving business transformation and achieving competitive advantage within the retail real estate sector.
