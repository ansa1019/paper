We operate in an environment characterized by both increasing complexity in global security and continuing economic pressures. A significant component of our strategy is to focus on program execution, improving the quality and predictability of the delivery of our products and services, and placing security capability quickly into the hands of our customers at affordable prices. We continue to focus on affordability initiatives and expect to invest in technologies to fulfill new mission requirements for our customers. Our business strategy includes strengthening our relationships internationally through partnerships and joint technology efforts. We have ongoing programs for sustainment activities, which include significant investments in information technology to support programs and general enterprise information technology infrastructure. The majority of our capital expenditures are for equipment, facilities infrastructure, and information technology, including costs for the development or purchase of internal-use software. Our investments and acquisitions have sought to align our businesses with critical national priorities and mission areas. We routinely conduct discussions, evaluate targets, and enter into agreements regarding possible acquisitions, divestitures, ventures, and equity investments. We have divested our former information systems & global solutions business, which reflects a strategic shift that had a major effect on our operations and financial results. Our aeronautics business segment is engaged in the research, design, development, manufacture, integration, sustainment, support, and upgrade of advanced military aircraft, which includes significant IT capabilities to enhance operational efficiency and support business needs. We have a balanced cash deployment strategy to enhance stockholder value and position ourselves to take advantage of new business opportunities when they arise, which includes investing in our people to ensure we have the technical skills necessary to succeed. Our organizational capabilities are enhanced through the integration of technology resources, which are critical for maintaining competitive advantage in the market. We expect our cash from operations will continue to be sufficient to support our operations and anticipated capital expenditures for the foreseeable future. We have generated strong operating cash flows, which have been the primary source of funding for our operations, capital expenditures, debt service, and repayments. We have access to credit markets for liquidity or general corporate purposes. The investments we have made in recent years have sought to align our businesses with what we believe are the most critical national priorities and mission areas, including the development of advanced technologies and cybersecurity services. Our focus on program execution and the integration of technology capabilities into our operational processes is essential for responding to evolving market dynamics and customer needs. We continue to monitor the financial market environment and actively manage counterparty exposure to minimize potential impacts from adverse developments. Our strategic management practices include leveraging IT capabilities to enhance our operational flexibility and market responsiveness, ensuring that we can adapt to changes in the business environment effectively.
