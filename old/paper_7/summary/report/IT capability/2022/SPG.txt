The company operates as a self-administered and self-managed real estate investment trust, focusing on maximizing total financial return through the enhancement of profitability and operation of its properties and investments. This includes attracting and retaining high-quality tenants, utilizing economies of scale to reduce operating expenses, and selectively acquiring or increasing interests in high-quality real estate assets. The company also generates consumer traffic through marketing initiatives and strategic corporate alliances, which are essential for maintaining competitive advantage in the retail environment. The operational strategy emphasizes the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with other capabilities to support business needs and enhance organizational performance. The company routinely reviews and evaluates acquisition opportunities based on their ability to enhance the portfolio, which reflects a focus on strategic resource allocation and operational flexibility. The impact of the COVID-19 pandemic necessitated a reevaluation of operational practices, leading to actions aimed at reducing costs and increasing liquidity, which underscores the importance of effective management capability in navigating market dynamics. The company’s financing strategy relies primarily on long-term fixed-rate debt, with a focus on maintaining sufficient flexibility to access capital in various forms, including credit facilities and commercial paper programs. This financial structure supports the company's growth initiatives, including redevelopment and expansion projects that enhance profitability and market share. The emphasis on digital technologies and innovation partnerships is evident in the company's approach to establishing its malls as leading market resource providers, which includes the integration of payment systems and digital media initiatives. The company’s international strategy involves partnering with established real estate companies and financing investments with local currency to mitigate foreign exchange risks, reflecting a comprehensive approach to resource management and operational effectiveness. The company’s ability to adapt to changing business needs and directions, particularly in the context of market responsiveness and competitive positioning, is critical for sustaining its operational efficiency and achieving performance gains. The strategic management practices employed by the company are designed to align with organizational goals and enhance overall business value, emphasizing the importance of information management and data analytics in decision-making processes. The company’s focus on high-quality real estate across the retail spectrum, coupled with its commitment to maintaining its REIT status, demonstrates a proactive approach to managing its investment portfolio and responding to environmental opportunities. The integration of technology capabilities into the company’s operations is essential for optimizing processes and enhancing customer relationships, ultimately contributing to improved firm performance and market agility.
