Our business strategy is designed to address key challenges facing our company, including a highly competitive operating environment and a rapidly changing retail landscape, including the growth in e-commerce. To adapt to these challenges, we intend to continue to focus on becoming faster, stronger, and better by leveraging scale and technology in global markets across our operations. This includes developing and scaling core capabilities through technology and building differentiated talent and culture. Our 2019 productivity plan will leverage new technology and business models to further simplify, harmonize, and automate processes; re-engineer our go-to-market and information systems; and optimize our manufacturing and supply chain footprint. We have also committed to investing in technology resources to enhance our operational efficiency and support our business transformation initiatives. Our risk management framework involves leveraging an integrated risk management approach, which includes oversight of key aspects of the company's business, including risk assessment and risk mitigation related to technology deployment and cybersecurity. We continue to monitor changes in the retail landscape and to identify actions we may take to build our global e-commerce and digital capabilities. Our operations are supported by a robust IT architecture that facilitates the coordination of our assets and internal processes, enhancing our ability to respond to market dynamics and consumer demands. The integration of technology into our business processes is critical for achieving operational flexibility and improving firm performance. We recognize that our IT capabilities are essential in transforming our organization into a competitive force, enabling us to mobilize and deploy IT-based resources effectively. Our focus on information management capability allows us to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, and security, tailored to respond to changing business needs. The assessment of our indefinite-lived intangible assets and goodwill requires significant management judgment, particularly in evaluating the impact of competitive operating and macroeconomic factors on future levels of sales, operating profit, and cash flows. We have implemented various technology functionalities to enhance our information processing capacity and improve our decision-making speed. Our investment in digital technologies is aimed at optimizing our business processes and enhancing customer relationships, which are vital for maintaining market agility and competitive advantage. We are committed to continuous improvement in our technology capabilities to ensure alignment with our strategic objectives and to drive innovation across our operations. The integration of digital infrastructure into our business model is expected to yield performance gains and support our long-term sustainable growth objectives. As we navigate the complexities of the global market, our ability to leverage IT capabilities effectively will be crucial in achieving our organizational goals and enhancing our overall business value.
