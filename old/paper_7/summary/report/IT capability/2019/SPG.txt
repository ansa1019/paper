Simon Property Group, Inc. operates as a self-administered and self-managed real estate investment trust, focusing on maximizing total financial return through the acquisition, development, and management of premier shopping, dining, and entertainment destinations. The company seeks to enhance profitability and operational efficiency by attracting high-quality tenants, utilizing economies of scale, and generating consumer traffic through strategic corporate alliances and marketing initiatives. Simon's strategy includes selectively acquiring or increasing interests in high-quality real estate assets, which aligns with its resource allocation and investment decisions aimed at enhancing market-based performance. The firm emphasizes the importance of operational flexibility and the ability to respond to market dynamics, which is critical for maintaining competitive advantage in the retail real estate sector. 

The company also engages in redevelopment and expansion projects to enhance the profitability and market share of existing assets, indicating a focus on business transformation and innovation. Simon's international strategy involves partnering with established real estate companies and financing investments with local currency to mitigate foreign exchange risk, demonstrating a commitment to effective resource management and strategic resource allocation. The firm’s financial activities are supported by a robust capital strategy that includes maintaining sufficient flexibility to access various forms of capital, thereby ensuring operational efficiency and the ability to fund growth initiatives.

Simon also monitors external influences and market responsiveness, which are essential for navigating competitive actions and maintaining its position within the industry.
