Duke Energy's management emphasizes the importance of transforming the customer experience to meet changing customer expectations through enhanced convenience, control, and choice in energy supply and usage. The modernization of the power grid is aimed at improving reliability and flexibility in support of increased distributed energy sources. Duke Energy's strategic initiatives focus on operational excellence through engagement with employees and being one of the best safety performers in the industry. The company is committed to cleaner, smarter energy solutions that customers value, which involves significant investments in technology and infrastructure. In 2015, Duke Energy advanced several strategic initiatives, including the acquisition of Piedmont Natural Gas, which reflects the growing importance of natural gas to the future of energy infrastructure. The acquisition is expected to establish a broader gas infrastructure platform within Duke Energy, enhancing its operational flexibility and capability to respond to market dynamics. The company plans to invest between $4 billion and $5 billion annually in regulated utilities business growth projects, which will support long-term adjusted earnings growth and the ability to provide affordable, reliable energy. Duke Energy's operational strategies include investments in renewable projects to respond to customer interest in clean energy resources while increasing diversity in the generation portfolio. The company is also focused on achieving operational efficiencies and productivity improvements, which are critical for maintaining competitive advantage in a rapidly evolving market.
