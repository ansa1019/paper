PSEG's business plan is designed to achieve growth while managing risks associated with fluctuating commodity prices and changes in customer demand, focusing on operational excellence, financial strength, and disciplined investment. The company emphasizes operational performance while developing opportunities in both competitive and regulated businesses. In 2015, PSEG's total nuclear fleet achieved an average capacity factor of 90%, and diverse fuel mix and dispatch flexibility allowed it to generate approximately 55 TWh while addressing unit outages and balancing fuel availability and price volatility. PSEG's strategy includes maintaining and expanding a reliable generation fleet with the flexibility to utilize a diverse mix of fuels, which allows it to respond to market volatility and capitalize on opportunities as they arise. The company continues to invest in transmission and distribution infrastructure projects aimed at maintaining service reliability and bolstering system resiliency. PSEG's operational flexibility and disciplined investment approach have yielded strong results, allowing it to effectively deploy capital without the need for additional equity. The focus on capital spending for projects that yield contemporaneous returns has been a key driver of financial performance, with significant investments made in the Energy Strong program and gas system modernization. PSEG's investments reflect a recognition of the value of opportunistic growth in the power business, and the company actively monitors regulatory and legislative developments that impact operational excellence and financial strength. The firm’s IT capabilities have evolved to encompass the coordination of assets and internal processes, enhancing its ability to support business strategies and work processes. PSEG's management emphasizes the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities to transform the firm into a competitive force. The company’s IT governance and management practices are integral to achieving operational efficiency and organizational effectiveness, which are essential for navigating market dynamics and regulatory contexts. PSEG's strategic decisions are informed by a focus on information management capability, ensuring that data and information are provided to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, and security. The firm’s ability to tailor its IT resources in response to changing business needs and directions is critical for maintaining competitive advantage and enhancing firm performance. The company’s approach to investment decisions reflects a commitment to aligning technology capabilities with organizational goals, thereby fostering innovation and improving performance metrics. PSEG's operational and financial performance is underpinned by a solid balance sheet and positive cash flow, which are essential for funding capital expenditures and shareholder dividend payments.
