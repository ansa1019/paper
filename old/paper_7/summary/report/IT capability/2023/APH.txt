The harsh environment solutions segment designs, manufactures, and markets a broad range of ruggedized interconnect products for various end markets, including information technology and data communications, highlighting the importance of IT capabilities in these applications. The communications solutions segment also emphasizes the development of high-technology performance-enhancing solutions, which are essential for maintaining operational efficiency and meeting evolving customer demands. The company’s strategy includes pursuing broad market diversification and fostering collaborative, entrepreneurial management, which are critical for leveraging IT resources and capabilities to support business needs and enhance firm performance.
