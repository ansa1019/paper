PSEG's business plan is designed to achieve growth while managing risks associated with fluctuating commodity prices and changes in customer demand, focusing on operational excellence, financial strength, and disciplined investment. The company emphasizes operational performance while developing opportunities in both competitive and regulated businesses, with flexibility in its generating fleet allowing it to take advantage of opportunities in a rapidly evolving market. PSEG continues to invest in transmission projects that focus on reliability improvements and replacement of aging infrastructure, which are critical for maintaining service reliability to customers and bolstering system resiliency. The company has implemented energy efficiency and demand response programs, which are regulated by the New Jersey Board of Public Utilities (BPU). PSEG's investments in technology infrastructure are aimed at enhancing operational efficiency and supporting business innovation. The focus on disciplined investment includes areas that complement existing business operations and provide reasonable risk-adjusted returns, such as upgrading energy infrastructure and responding to trends in environmental protection. PSEG's strategic initiatives include improving utility operations through growth in investment in transmission and distribution (T&D) and other infrastructure projects designed to enhance system reliability and resiliency. The company has recognized the importance of mobilizing and deploying IT-based resources in combination with leveraging the value of other resources and capabilities to transform into a competitive force. PSEG's operational flexibility is enhanced through investments in technology capabilities that facilitate communication and improve decision-making speed. The company continues to monitor and engage with stakeholders on significant regulatory and legislative developments, which are crucial for maintaining its competitive positioning in the market. PSEG's ability to recover costs associated with its capital investments and ensure fair and timely rate relief from regulatory bodies is essential for its financial performance. The company is also focused on managing its energy obligations and re-contracting its open supply positions in response to changes in demand, which reflects its commitment to operational excellence and strategic resource allocation. PSEG's approach to investment decisions is guided by rigorous criteria, ensuring that capital is deployed effectively to enhance organizational capabilities and achieve business objectives. The integration of digital technologies and the development of a robust IT architecture are pivotal in supporting PSEG's business processes and enhancing its overall performance. The company recognizes the need for ongoing investment in IT capabilities to adapt to market dynamics and consumer demands, thereby ensuring its resilience and competitiveness in the energy sector.
