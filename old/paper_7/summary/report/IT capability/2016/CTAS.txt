Cintas helps more than 900,000 businesses of all types and sizes by providing a wide range of products and services that enhance customers' image and help keep their facilities and employees clean and safe. The company’s strategy is to achieve revenue growth by increasing penetration at existing customers and broadening its customer base, which includes evaluating strategic acquisitions as opportunities arise. The combination of Cintas' distribution system and strong customer relationships provides a platform for launching additional products and services. Cintas evaluates operating segment performance based on revenue and income before income taxes, regularly reviewing its operating segments for reporting purposes based on information its chief operating decision maker reviews for resource allocation and performance assessment. The company’s fiscal 2016 total revenue was $4.9 billion, an increase of 9.6% over the prior fiscal year, driven by organic growth from increased sales volume and acquisitions. The uniform rental and facility services reportable operating segment revenue increased 6.7%, attributed to new business growth and improved sales representative productivity, which is enhanced through increased tenure and training. The gross margin for this segment improved due to factors including new business sold, penetration of additional products into existing customers, and improved efficiency of internal processes. Selling and administrative expenses increased primarily due to labor and employee-partner related expenses. The first aid and safety services reportable operating segment revenue increased significantly, driven by increased sales volume and acquisitions, although gross margin decreased due to integration costs. Cintas’ ability to mobilize and deploy IT-based resources in combination with leveraging other resources and capabilities is crucial for transforming the firm into a competitive force. The company regularly assesses its IT capabilities to ensure they effectively support business needs and enhance operational efficiency. Cintas’ management emphasizes the importance of information management capability, which includes providing data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, and access. The integration of technology resources and management skills is vital for achieving organizational goals and enhancing business value. Cintas continues to invest in technology and innovation to improve its operational flexibility and market responsiveness, thereby enhancing its competitive advantage. The company’s strategic resource allocation decisions are influenced by the need to adapt to changing business environments and consumer demands. Cintas' focus on improving internal processes and leveraging technology capabilities supports its overall business strategy and enhances its performance outcomes. The company’s commitment to continuous improvement in operational efficiency and strategic alignment with business objectives is evident in its ongoing evaluations of performance metrics and investment choices.
