Mastercard is a technology company in the global payments industry that connects consumers, financial institutions, merchants, governments, digital partners, businesses, and other organizations worldwide, enabling them to use electronic forms of payment instead of cash and checks. We make payments easier and more efficient by creating a wide range of payment solutions and services using our family of well-known brands. Our payment solutions are designed to ensure safety and security for the global payments system. We facilitate the switching of payment transactions and deliver related products and services through our core global payments processing network. We also provide value-added offerings such as safety and security products, information and analytics services, consulting, loyalty and reward programs, and issuer and acquirer processing. Our continued investment in strategic initiatives and higher operating costs is primarily driven by our focus on enhancing our information management capability, which includes the ability to provide data and information to users with appropriate levels of accuracy, timeliness, reliability, security, confidentiality, connectivity, and access. Our operational efficiency is supported by the deployment of IT resources that enhance our business processes and capabilities. The increases in operating expenses were primarily related to our continued investment in areas such as real-time account-based payments, digital services, data analytics, and geographic expansion, which are essential for maintaining our competitive advantage in the market. Our personnel expenses increased significantly due to a higher number of employees to support these strategic developments. Data processing and telecommunications expenses also rose due to capacity growth of our business, indicating a direct correlation between our IT capability and operational demands. The effective income tax rate was influenced by a more favorable geographic mix of earnings, which is indicative of our strategic resource allocation and management capability.
