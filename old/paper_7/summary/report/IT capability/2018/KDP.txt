The company believes that its integrated business model enables greater flexibility and responsiveness to the changing needs of large retail customers, allowing for the leveraging of scale and reduction of costs through enhanced geographic manufacturing and distribution coverage. The North American beverage market is influenced by key trends and uncertainties, including increased health consciousness and changes in consumer preferences, which have created category headwinds for several products.
