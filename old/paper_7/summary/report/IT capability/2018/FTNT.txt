Fortinet is a global leader in cybersecurity solutions designed to provide broad visibility and segmentation of the digital attack surface through its integrated security fabric platform, which features automated protection, detection, and responses. The Fortinet security fabric platform is an architectural approach that protects the entire digital attack surface, including network core, endpoints, applications, data centers, and private and public cloud. This platform enables disparate security devices to work together as an integrated, automated, and collaborative solution, thereby enhancing operational efficiency and supporting business needs.
