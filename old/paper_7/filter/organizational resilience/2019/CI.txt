organizational efficiency plan as discussed in note 15 to the consolidated financial statements, during the fourth quarter of 2019 the company committed to a plan to increase our organizational alignment and operational efficiency and reduce costs. as a result we recognized a charge in selling, general and administrative expenses of $207 million, pre-tax ($162 million, after-tax) in the fourth quarter of 2019. we expect to realize annualized after-tax savings of approximately $180 million. a substantial portion of the savings is expected to be realized in 2020.
